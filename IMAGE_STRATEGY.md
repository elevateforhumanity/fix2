## Image Specifications

### Hero Banners
- Dimensions: 1920x1080px (16:9)
- Format: PNG or JPG
- Quality: High (90-100%)
- Style: Professional, diverse, action-oriented

### Program Cards
- Dimensions: 800x600px (4:3)
- Format: JPG
- Quality: Medium-High (80-90%)
- Style: Show actual training environments

### Section Images
- Dimensions: 1200x800px (3:2)
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Quality: Medium-High (80-90%)
- Style: Authentic, relatable, diverse

### Icons/Logos
- Dimensions: 512x512px (1:1)
- Format: PNG with transparency
- Quality: High (100%)
- Style: Clean, professional
