Sub-Office Onboarding

Step 1: Review Agreement

Review the Sub-Office Memorandum of Understanding. This outlines compensation, responsibilities, and compliance standards.

Step 2: Sign & Submit

  • Download the PDF version
  • Sign electronically or manually
  • Email or upload the signed copy

Step 3: Training & Access

  • Complete onboarding training
  • Receive intake procedures
  • Receive software access credentials
Important: Sub-Offices are compensated only on the base preparation fee. All add-on fees are handled centrally.

Step 4: Begin Intake

Once approved, you may begin client intake following Main Office procedures.
