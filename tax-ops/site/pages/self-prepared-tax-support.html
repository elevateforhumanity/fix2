Self-Prepared Tax Support

If you prepare your own taxes using software such as TurboTax, Cash App, or FreeTaxUSA, we offer paid review and consultation services. These services do not include filing your return under our EFIN.

Available Services

Tax Return Review

$200 flat

  • Review of completed return
  • Identification of errors or missed credits
  • Explanation of findings

Tax Consultation (Hourly)

$125 per hour (1-hour minimum)

  • Questions about income, dependents, or credits
  • Planning or clarification before filing

Guided Self-Filing Support

$300 flat

  • Step-by-step guidance while you file
  • No submission under our EFIN

Credit-Only Review

$150 flat

  • Child Tax Credit, EITC, education credits
  • Eligibility review only

Important: Review and consultation services do not constitute tax preparation or filing. Clients remain responsible for submitting their own returns.

If you choose to upgrade to full tax preparation, your review fee will be credited toward the preparation cost.
