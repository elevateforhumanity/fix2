# Sub-Office Tax Preparation Memorandum of Understanding

**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]

## 1. Relationship

This Memorandum of Understanding ("MOU") establishes the working relationship between
**[Main Office Name]** ("Main Office") and approved Sub-Office operators ("Sub-Office").
This agreement does not constitute a partnership, joint venture, or ownership interest.

## 2. Scope of Work

### Sub-Office Responsibilities

- Client intake and document collection
- Accurate data entry and preliminary preparation
- Compliance with Main Office procedures

### Main Office Responsibilities

- Software licensing and systems (Drake)
- Pricing structure and fee determination
- Final review and filing authority
- Compliance oversight, corrections, notices, and audits

## 3. Compensation

- Sub-Office receives **45% of the base preparation fee** per accepted return
- Main Office retains **55% of the base preparation fee**
- Compensation applies only to accepted returns

**All add-on fees**, including additional forms, Schedule C, multiple W-2s, dependents,
complexity charges, and processing fees, are retained **100% by the Main Office**.

## 4. Exclusions

Compensation does not apply to:

- Rejected or withdrawn returns
- Penalties or interest
- Audit or notice response work
- Refund advances or third-party incentives

## 5. Quality & Compliance

Sub-Offices must maintain acceptable accuracy and compliance standards.
Excessive errors or violations may result in removal from bonus eligibility or termination.

## 6. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with written notice. Accepted returns completed
prior to termination will be paid according to this agreement.
