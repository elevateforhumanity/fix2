# COMPLETE TOP LMS PLATFORMS ANALYSIS
## Comparing Against: Coursera, edX, LinkedIn Learning, Pluralsight, Canvas, Moodle

---

## 1. COURSERA - Consumer Learning Leader

### What They Do Well:
✅ **Homepage:**
- Full-width hero with large imagery
- Clear value proposition: "Learn from 350+ universities"
- Category browsing with icons
- Partner logos (Yale, Google, IBM, etc.)
- Trending courses carousel
- Student testimonials with photos

✅ **Course Pages:**
- Large video player (70% of screen)
- Course syllabus expandable
- Instructor bio with credentials
- Related courses section
- "What you'll learn" bullets
- Student reviews and ratings

✅ **Video Player:**
- Playback speed controls
- Subtitles/captions
- Progress tracking
- Auto-advance to next lesson
- Download for offline

✅ **Dashboard:**
- Continue learning section
- Upcoming deadlines
- Progress tracking
- Achievements/certificates

### What We're Missing:
❌ Partner logos carousel
❌ Trending courses section
❌ Instructor bios with photos
❌ Student reviews/ratings
❌ Video transcripts
❌ Downloadable resources
