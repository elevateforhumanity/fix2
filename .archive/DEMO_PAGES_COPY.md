## üìù Tone & Voice Guidelines

### /demo Page (Platform Store)
**Tone:** Professional, confident, B2B
**Audience:** Decision-makers with budget authority
**Goal:** Convert to sales call

**Do:**
- Use specific numbers and metrics
- Mention funding sources
- Emphasize time/cost savings
- Show social proof (if available)

**Don't:**
- Use student-facing language
- Hide pricing or make it confusing
- Oversell or use hype

- Make demos hard to find
