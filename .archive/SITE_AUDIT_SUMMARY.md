## üì∏ How to Get Better Images

### Option 1: Take Your Own (RECOMMENDED)
**Cost:** FREE
**Equipment:** Modern smartphone
**Time:** 1-2 hours

**What to Photograph:**
- Students actively learning (get permission)
- Instructors teaching
- Hands-on training activities
- Your facilities
- Equipment and tools
- Graduation ceremonies
- Success stories

**Photography Tips:**
- Use natural light (near windows)
- Shoot during daytime
- Keep subjects in focus
- Show action and activity
- Capture diverse students
- Get multiple angles

### Option 2: Hire a Photographer
**Cost:** $500-1500
**Best for:** Professional hero images, headshots
**Find:** Local photographers on Thumbtack, Upwork

### Option 3: Better Stock Photos (Quick Fix)
**Cost:** FREE (Unsplash, Pexels) or $30/month (Shutterstock)
**Search for:**
- "vocational training bright"
- "apprenticeship workshop diverse"
- "career education hands-on"
- Look for bright, clear, action-oriented images
