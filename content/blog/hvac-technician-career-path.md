# HVAC Technician Career Path

With Indiana's extreme weather, HVAC technicians are always in demand. Our program prepares you for EPA certification and entry-level positions.

## Training Includes

- Residential and commercial systems
- Refrigeration principles
- Electrical fundamentals
- EPA 608 certification prep
- Customer service skills

## Earnings Potential

- Entry-level: $35,000-$45,000
- Experienced: $50,000-$70,000
- Master technicians: $70,000+

## Program Length

6-9 months, hands-on training with local HVAC companies.
