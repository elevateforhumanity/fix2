# Peer Recovery Coach Certification

Peer Recovery Coaches use their own recovery experience to support others on their journey. This rewarding career combines personal growth with professional service.

## Training Components

- Ethics and boundaries
- Recovery support strategies
- Trauma-informed care
- Motivational interviewing
- Crisis intervention

## Employment Settings

- Treatment centers
- Recovery community organizations
- Hospitals and clinics
- Correctional facilities
- Community mental health

## Compensation

$15-$22/hour with benefits. High job satisfaction and personal fulfillment.
