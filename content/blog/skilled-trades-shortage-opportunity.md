# The Skilled Trades Shortage

America faces a massive skilled trades shortage. 2 million positions unfilled. This is your opportunity.

## The Numbers

- 75% of contractors can't find qualified workers
- Average age of tradespeople: 55
- Retirement wave creating openings
- Starting wages: $40,000-$60,000

## Why the Shortage?

- Decades of "college for everyone" messaging
- Aging workforce retiring
- Construction boom
- Manufacturing resurgence

## The Opportunity

High demand = high pay + job security + career growth.
