# Indiana Job Market Analysis 2024

Indiana's economy is strong, but opportunities aren't evenly distributed. Here's where the jobs are.

## Top Growth Sectors

1. **Healthcare**: 15% growth, 50,000+ openings
2. **Logistics**: 12% growth, driven by e-commerce
3. **Advanced Manufacturing**: 8% growth, automation-resistant roles
4. **Construction**: 10% growth, skilled trades shortage
5. **Technology**: 14% growth, especially cybersecurity

## Highest-Demand Occupations

- Registered Nurses
- CDL Drivers
- HVAC Technicians
- Software Developers
- Welders

## Wage Trends

Median wages up 4.2% year-over-year. Skilled trades seeing fastest growth.
