# How to Apply for WIOA Funding

WIOA can fully fund your training. Here's exactly how to apply.

## Step 1: Visit WorkOne

Find your local WorkOne Career Center. Bring:
- Photo ID
- Social Security card
- Proof of income (if applicable)
- High school diploma/GED

## Step 2: Meet with Career Advisor

They'll assess your eligibility and career goals.

## Step 3: Complete Assessment

Skills assessment determines training needs.

## Step 4: Develop Employment Plan

Your advisor creates an Individual Employment Plan (IEP).

## Step 5: Get Approved

Approval typically takes 1-2 weeks.

## Step 6: Enroll in Training

Choose from approved programs (like ours!).

## Tips for Success

- Be honest about your situation
- Show commitment to completion
- Follow up regularly
- Attend all appointments
