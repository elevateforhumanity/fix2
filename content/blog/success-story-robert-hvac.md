# Career Switcher at 38

Robert managed a retail store for 12 years. Burned out and underpaid, he took a chance on HVAC training.

## The Switch

- 6-month HVAC program
- EPA certification
- Apprenticeship with local company
- $52,000 first-year earnings

## Why He's Happy

"I work with my hands, solve problems, and make good money. No more retail stress."

## For Career Changers

"Age doesn't matter. Skills matter. Get trained and go."
