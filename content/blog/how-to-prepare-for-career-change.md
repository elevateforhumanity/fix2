# Preparing for Career Change

Changing careers is scary but possible. Here's how to do it right.

## Step 1: Assess Your Situation

- Why do you want to change?
- What are your transferable skills?
- What's your financial runway?
- What are your constraints (family, location)?

## Step 2: Research Options

- What fields interest you?
- What's the job market like?
- What training is required?
- What's the earning potential?

## Step 3: Get Trained

Invest in quality training. Credentials matter.

## Step 4: Build Your Network

Connect with people in your target field.

## Step 5: Gain Experience

Volunteer, intern, or take entry-level work.
