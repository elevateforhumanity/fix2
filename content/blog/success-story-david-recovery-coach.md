# From Addiction to Advocate

David struggled with addiction for 15 years. Five years sober, he's now a certified peer recovery coach helping others.

## Finding Purpose

"I wanted to turn my pain into purpose," David shares. Our peer recovery coach program gave him the tools.

## Making a Difference

David works at a treatment center, supporting 20-30 clients. "When someone tells me I helped save their life, that's everything."

## The Ripple Effect

"Recovery gave me my life back. This career lets me give that gift to others."
