# Professional Esthetician Training

The beauty and wellness industry is booming. Estheticians provide skincare services in spas, salons, medical offices, and their own businesses.

## Curriculum

- Facial treatments and techniques
- Chemical peels and microdermabrasion
- Makeup application
- Hair removal (waxing, threading)
- Product knowledge and retail

## Licensure

700-hour program meets Indiana licensing requirements. Includes state board exam prep.

## Income Potential

$30,000-$50,000 annually, plus tips and retail commissions.
