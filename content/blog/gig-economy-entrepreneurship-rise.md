# The Gig Economy Revolution

36% of Americans now do gig work. This shift is creating new opportunities for entrepreneurship.

## The Trend

- Uber, DoorDash, Upwork normalizing self-employment
- Lower barriers to starting businesses
- Technology enabling solo entrepreneurs
- Desire for flexibility and autonomy

## Opportunities

- Service businesses (barber, esthetician, tax prep)
- Skilled trades (HVAC, plumbing, electrical)
- Consulting and coaching
- E-commerce and digital products

## The Skills You Need

- Business planning
- Marketing
- Financial management
- Customer service
