# Unemployed to $65K Annually

James lost his factory job when the plant closed. At 45, he thought his career was over. Then he discovered CDL training.

## Quick Turnaround

- 4 weeks of training
- Class A CDL earned
- $8,000 sign-on bonus
- $65,000 first-year earnings

## Why It Worked

"The training was intense but practical. Within a month, I was driving for a major carrier."

## Advice to Others

"Don't think you're too old to start over. I wish I'd done this years ago."
