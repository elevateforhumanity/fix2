# Barber Apprenticeship: Your Path to a Licensed Career

The barbering industry is experiencing a renaissance. With the rise of specialty barbershops and grooming culture, skilled barbers are in high demand across Indiana. Our Barber Apprenticeship program provides a direct path to state licensure and a rewarding career.

## Program Overview

Our 1,500-hour apprenticeship meets all Indiana State Board of Barber Examiners requirements. You'll learn:

- **Fundamental Skills**: Haircutting, shaving, beard trimming, and styling techniques
- **Sanitation & Safety**: Proper sterilization, infection control, and workplace safety
- **Business Skills**: Client relations, appointment management, and retail sales
- **State Board Prep**: Comprehensive preparation for written and practical exams

## Hands-On Learning

Unlike traditional classroom-only programs, our apprenticeship model places you in a real barbershop from day one. You'll work alongside licensed professionals, serving actual clients and building your portfolio.

**Training Schedule:**
- 30-40 hours per week
- 9-12 month completion timeline
- Flexible scheduling options available

## Career Outcomes

Graduates of our program have gone on to:
- Open their own barbershops
- Work in high-end salons and specialty shops
- Build loyal client bases earning $40,000-$70,000+ annually
- Compete in barbering competitions

## Funding Options

This program is eligible for:
- WIOA funding (fully covered for eligible students)
- Workforce Ready Grant
- Employer sponsorship
- Payment plans

## Requirements

- 18 years or older
- High school diploma or equivalent
- Valid Indiana driver's license or ID
- Pass background check
- Commitment to full program completion
