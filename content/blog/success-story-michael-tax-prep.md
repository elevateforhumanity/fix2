# Seasonal to Year-Round Success

Michael took our tax preparation course to earn extra money during tax season. Three years later, he owns an accounting firm.

## The Evolution

- Started with 20 clients
- Grew to 200+ clients
- Added bookkeeping services
- Hired 2 employees

## Income Growth

Year 1: $15,000 (seasonal)
Year 2: $45,000 (seasonal + bookkeeping)
Year 3: $85,000 (full-time business)

## His Secret

"Quality service and word-of-mouth. Happy clients refer their friends."
