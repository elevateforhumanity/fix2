# Healthcare: Indiana's Career Engine

Healthcare is Indiana's largest employer and fastest-growing sector. Here's why it's a smart career choice.

## The Growth

- 50,000+ openings annually
- 15% growth through 2030
- Aging population driving demand
- Pandemic highlighted critical need

## Entry Points

You don't need a 4-year degree:
- CNA: 4-6 weeks
- Medical Assistant: 12 weeks
- Phlebotomy: 8 weeks
- EKG Tech: 6 weeks

## Career Ladders

Start as CNA → LPN → RN → BSN. Many employers offer tuition assistance.

## Job Security

Healthcare is recession-proof. People always need medical care.
