# Direct Support Professional Training

Direct Support Professionals (DSPs) play a vital role in helping individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities live independently and participate fully in their communities.

## What You'll Learn

- Person-centered care planning
- Medication administration
- Crisis intervention techniques
- Documentation and reporting
- Rights and dignity of individuals served

## Career Path

DSPs work in residential facilities, day programs, and community settings. Starting salary: $15-18/hour with growth to $20+/hour for experienced professionals.

## Program Details

- 120 hours of training
- State certification included
- Job placement assistance
- Fully funded for eligible students
