# Healthcare Certifications Fast-Track

Healthcare is Indiana's largest employer. Our certification programs get you working quickly in this stable, growing field.

## Available Certifications

- Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA)
- Phlebotomy Technician
- EKG Technician
- Medical Assistant
- Patient Care Technician

## Why Healthcare?

- Job security
- Competitive pay ($14-$22/hour starting)
- Career advancement opportunities
- Meaningful work

## Training Format

2-12 weeks depending on certification. Clinical rotations included.
