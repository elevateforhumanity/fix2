# Succeeding in Your Apprenticeship

Apprenticeships are earn-while-you-learn opportunities. Here's how to excel.

## 1. Show Up On Time

Punctuality shows professionalism and respect.

## 2. Ask Questions

No question is stupid. Master craftspeople want to teach.

## 3. Take Notes

Document techniques and tips. Build your reference library.

## 4. Practice Outside Work

The more you practice, the faster you improve.

## 5. Accept Feedback

Criticism helps you grow. Don't take it personally.

## 6. Build Relationships

Your mentor and coworkers are your network.

## 7. Stay Safe

Follow all safety protocols. Injuries end careers.

## 8. Be Reliable

Show up, work hard, finish tasks.

## 9. Study for Exams

Licensure requires passing tests. Prepare thoroughly.

## 10. Think Long-Term

Every day is building your career foundation.
