# Beauty School Dream Realized

Maria worked retail for 10 years, dreaming of a career in skincare. Our esthetician program made it happen.

## The Path

- 700-hour esthetician program
- State licensure
- Started in a spa
- Opened her own business after 2 years

## Business Success

Maria's spa now has 5 treatment rooms and 3 employees. "I never imagined I could own my own business."

## Her Message

"If you're passionate about something, invest in yourself. The training pays off."
