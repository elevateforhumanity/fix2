# Finding Purpose as a DSP

Jennifer worked in customer service for years, feeling unfulfilled. Becoming a Direct Support Professional changed everything.

## The Calling

"I wanted work that mattered," Jennifer explains. Our DSP program prepared her to support individuals with disabilities.

## The Reward

"Every day, I help someone live their best life. The pay is good, but the fulfillment is priceless."

## Career Growth

Jennifer started as a DSP, became a lead, and is now pursuing her social work degree.
