# Welcome to Elevate For Humanity

## Getting Started Is Simple

This platform helps you find training, apply for programs, and track your progress toward certification and employment.

**Everything you see here is ready to use right now.**

---

## Your First Steps

### 1. Explore Programs

Browse available training programs to find what matches your goals.

**What you'll see:**

- Clear eligibility requirements
- Program length and schedule
- Certifications you'll earn
- What happens after completion

**Look for:** Programs marked "Accepting Applications"

### 2. Check Your Eligibility

Each program page shows exactly what you need to qualify.

**Common requirements:**

- Age (usually 18+)
- Education level
- Background check (for some programs)
- Work authorization

**If you're not sure:** Contact the program coordinator listed on the page.

### 3. Complete Your Application

When you're ready to apply, the application will guide you step-by-step.

**You'll need:**

- Basic contact information
- Education history
- Work experience (if any)
- Identification documents

**How long it takes:** Most applications take 15-30 minutes.

### 4. Track Your Progress

Once enrolled, your dashboard shows:

- Your current courses
- Assignments and deadlines
- Hours completed
- Certifications earned

**Your dashboard is personal.** Only you and authorized staff can see it.

---

## Understanding Your Dashboard

### What You'll See

**When you first log in:**

- Welcome message with your name
- Current enrollment status
- Next steps to get started

**As you progress:**

- Course completion percentage
- Upcoming assignments
- Hours logged
- Skills mastered

### If Your Dashboard Is Empty

Don't worry. Your dashboard shows information as you create it.

**Empty sections mean:**

- You haven't enrolled in a course yet
- No assignments are due right now
- You haven't logged hours yet

**Each empty section tells you what to do next.**

1. **Clear title** - What this page is about

**You'll never see:**

- "Coming soon" messages

- Why it's locked

- Who can help you

## Application Process

### Before You Apply

**Make sure you have:**

- ✅ Read the full program description
- ✅ Checked eligibility requirements
- ✅ Gathered required documents
- ✅ Noted the application deadline

### During Application

**The system will:**

- Save your progress automatically
- Show which sections are complete
- Tell you what's still needed
- Confirm when you submit

**You can:**

- Save and come back later
- Edit before submitting
- Upload documents as you get them

### After You Apply

**You'll receive:**

- Confirmation email immediately
- Status updates as your application is reviewed
- Next steps if accepted
- Guidance if not accepted this time

**Timeline:**

- Most applications reviewed within 5-7 business days
- You'll be notified either way
- Acceptance includes enrollment instructions

## Common Questions

### "Why can't I see [feature]?"

If something isn't visible, it's either:

- Not available for your role
- Locked until you complete prerequisites
- Not part of your current program

**The page will explain which one and what to do.**

### "My dashboard is empty. Is something wrong?"

No. Your dashboard fills in as you:

- Enroll in programs
- Complete assignments
- Log hours
- Earn certifications

**Empty sections show what action creates data.**

### "How do I know if information is current?"

**All information on this site is current.**

If something changes:

- The page updates immediately
- You receive notification if it affects you
- Old information is never left visible

### "Can I trust what I see here?"

**Yes.**

This platform is built so that:

- Incomplete pages cannot be published
- Misleading information is blocked automatically
- All content is reviewed before going live
- Changes are tracked and verified

**If it's on the site, it's accurate.**
