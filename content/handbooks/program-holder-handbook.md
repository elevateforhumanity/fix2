## Your Responsibilities

### 1. Program Management

- Maintain active U.S. DOL Registered Apprenticeship status
- Follow approved work process schedules (1,500 hours minimum)
- Ensure students receive required classroom instruction
- Provide or arrange supervised hands-on training

### 2. Compliance & Recordkeeping

- Maintain accurate attendance and progress records
- Submit monthly progress reports to Elevate for Humanity
- Cooperate with audits and site visits
- Report any incidents, complaints, or safety concerns immediately

### 3. Student Support

- Provide clear expectations and program requirements
- Ensure safe, professional training environment
- Address student concerns promptly and professionally
- Support students in achieving competency milestones

### 4. Financial Integrity

- Accurate invoicing based on verified student progress
- No unauthorized charges or fees to students
- Transparent communication about program costs
- Cooperation with funding source requirements (WIOA, grants, etc.)

## Who Has Final Authority

**Elevate for Humanity** retains final authority over:

- Student enrollment eligibility

- Compliance determinations
- Partnership termination decisions

As Program Holder, you manage day-to-day operations, but Elevate for Humanity is the fiscal intermediary and maintains oversight responsibility.
