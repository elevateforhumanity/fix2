## Your Responsibilities

### 1. Student Oversight

- Conduct regular check-ins with assigned students (weekly or bi-weekly)
- Verify attendance and hours at training sites
- Monitor skill development and competency progress
- Provide coaching and support for struggling students
- Report student concerns or complaints immediately

### 2. Worksite Monitoring

- Conduct site visits to verify training quality
- Ensure worksites maintain safe, professional environments
- Verify hour logs match actual student attendance
- Report any compliance concerns to Elevate for Humanity
- Document site visit findings

### 3. Hour Log Verification

- Review and approve/reject hour logs weekly
- Verify hours match student attendance records
- Ensure skills logged match work process schedule
- Flag any discrepancies or concerns
- Approve logs only when verified

### 4. Compliance & Reporting

- Submit monthly reports to Elevate for Humanity
- Report any safety concerns, complaints, or violations immediately
- Maintain confidentiality of student records (FERPA)
- Cooperate with audits and oversight
- Follow all program policies and procedures

## Confidentiality & FERPA

As a Site Coordinator, you have access to **student education records** protected by FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act).

You **MUST**:

- Keep all student information confidential
- Only share student information with authorized personnel
- Secure all records (digital and physical)
- Report any data breaches immediately

You **MUST NOT**:

- Share student information with unauthorized persons
- Discuss student progress in public settings
- Post student information on social media
- Use student information for personal purposes

**FERPA violations can result in immediate termination and legal consequences.**
