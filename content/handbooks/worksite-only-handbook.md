## Your Responsibilities

### 1. Supervision & Training

- Provide direct supervision by licensed professionals
- Ensure students practice skills safely and correctly
- Provide constructive feedback on performance
- Follow approved work process schedules

### 2. Hour Logging

- Submit accurate hour logs weekly or bi-weekly
- Document specific skills practiced during each session
- Report any absences or issues immediately
- Cooperate with Site Coordinator verification

### 3. Safety & Professionalism

- Maintain clean, safe training environment
- Follow all OSHA and industry safety standards
- Treat students with respect and professionalism
- Report any incidents or concerns immediately

### 4. Compliance

- Maintain required business licenses and insurance
- Cooperate with site visits and audits
- Follow all program policies and procedures
- Do not make unauthorized promises to students
