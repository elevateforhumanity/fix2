## Template 1: DMCA Takedown Notice (For Hosting Providers)

**Use this when**: Someone copies your website content and hosts it elsewhere

```

To: [Hosting Provider Abuse Department]
Date: [Current Date]

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to report copyright infringement under the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA), 17 U.S.C. § 512(c).

IDENTIFICATION OF COPYRIGHTED WORK:
I am the authorized representative of Elevate for Humanity, the copyright owner of the following original works:

- Website content located at: https://www.elevateforhumanity.org
- Program descriptions, course materials, and educational content
- Website design, layout, and user interface elements
- Original images and media assets

IDENTIFICATION OF INFRINGING MATERIAL:
The following URL(s) contain unauthorized copies of our copyrighted material:

[List specific URLs here]

STATEMENT OF GOOD FAITH:
I have a good faith belief that the use of the copyrighted materials described above is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.

STATEMENT OF ACCURACY:
I swear, under penalty of perjury, that the information in this notification is accurate and that I am authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.

CONTACT INFORMATION:
Name: [Your Name]
Organization: Elevate for Humanity
Address: 8888 Keystone Crossing Suite 1300, Indianapolis, IN 46240
Email: [Your Email]
Phone: [Your Phone]

REQUESTED ACTION:
I request that you immediately remove or disable access to the infringing material listed above.

Signature: _______________________
Date: [Current Date]

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
Elevate for Humanity
```

## Template 2: Cease and Desist Letter (For Direct Contact)

**Use this when**: You know who copied your content and want them to stop

```
[Your Letterhead]

Date: [Current Date]

[Infringer's Name]
[Infringer's Address]

RE: Cease and Desist - Copyright Infringement

Dear [Infringer's Name]:

This letter serves as formal notice that you are infringing upon the intellectual property rights of Elevate for Humanity.

INFRINGEMENT DETAILS:
We have discovered that your website/platform at [URL] contains unauthorized copies of our copyrighted materials, including but not limited to:

1. [Specific content copied - e.g., "Program descriptions"]
2. [Specific content copied - e.g., "Course curriculum"]
3. [Specific content copied - e.g., "Website design elements"]

EVIDENCE:
Our original content was published on [date] at https://www.elevateforhumanity.org
Your infringing content was discovered on [date] at [their URL]

DEMAND:
We demand that you immediately:

1. Remove all infringing content from your website/platform
2. Cease all use of our copyrighted materials
3. Provide written confirmation of compliance within 10 business days
4. Destroy all copies of our materials in your possession

LEGAL CONSEQUENCES:
Failure to comply will result in:
- Filing a DMCA takedown notice with your hosting provider
- Pursuing legal action for copyright infringement
- Seeking monetary damages and attorney's fees
- Reporting to search engines for de-indexing

We prefer to resolve this matter amicably. Please contact us within 10 business days to confirm compliance.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
Elevate for Humanity
8888 Keystone Crossing Suite 1300
Indianapolis, IN 46240
[Your Email]
[Your Phone]
```

## Template 3: AI Training Opt-Out Notice

**Use this when**: An AI company scrapes your content despite robots.txt

```
Subject: Formal Opt-Out from AI Training - DMCA Notice

To: [AI Company Legal Department]
Date: [Current Date]

Dear Legal Team,

This letter serves as formal notice that Elevate for Humanity explicitly opts out of having our website content used for AI training, model development, or any machine learning purposes.

PROTECTED CONTENT:
All content on https://www.elevateforhumanity.org including:
- Text, images, and multimedia
- Program descriptions and course materials
- Website design and structure
- User-generated content

OPT-OUT MECHANISMS IN PLACE:
1. robots.txt explicitly blocks AI crawlers (GPTBot, CCBot, etc.)
2. X-Robots-Tag: noai, noimageai headers
3. Copyright notice on all pages
4. Terms of Service prohibiting AI training use

EVIDENCE OF VIOLATION:
[If applicable, describe how you discovered they used your content]

DEMAND:
We demand that you:
1. Immediately cease scraping our website
2. Remove our content from your training datasets
3. Exclude our domain from future training
4. Provide written confirmation within 14 days

LEGAL BASIS:
- Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA)
- Copyright Act of 1976
- Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA)
- Terms of Service violation

We reserve all rights and remedies available under law.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]
[Your Title]
Elevate for Humanity
```

## Important Notes

1. **Keep Records**: Save screenshots, archive pages, document dates
2. **Be Specific**: Always include exact URLs and descriptions
3. **Be Professional**: Angry letters are less effective
4. **Give Deadlines**: 10-14 business days is standard
5. **Follow Up**: If no response, escalate to hosting provider
6. **Consider Legal Counsel**: For high-value cases, hire an attorney

---

## Evidence Collection Checklist

Before sending any notice, collect:

- [ ] Screenshots of your original content (with timestamps)
- [ ] Screenshots of infringing content (with timestamps)
- [ ] Archive.org snapshots showing publication dates
- [ ] Server logs showing when you published
- [ ] Copyright registration (if you have one)
- [ ] Proof of your identity/authority
- [ ] Contact information for infringer
- [ ] Contact information for hosting provider
