## NAVIGATION HIERARCHY RULES

### Header (Primary Navigation)
- **Purpose:** Most important user journeys
- **Limit:** 6-7 main items
- **Audience:** All visitors
- **Goal:** Drive conversions (Apply, Enroll, Hire)

### Footer (Comprehensive Navigation)
- **Purpose:** Complete site map
- **Limit:** No limit, organize well
- **Audience:** Users looking for specific pages
- **Goal:** Make everything discoverable

### Utility Bar (Quick Access)
- **Purpose:** Contact, help, nonprofit info
- **Limit:** 4-5 items
- **Audience:** Users needing immediate help
- **Goal:** Build trust, provide support

### Search (Universal Access)
- **Purpose:** Find anything quickly
- **Limit:** N/A
- **Audience:** Users who know what they want
- **Goal:** Instant access to any page
