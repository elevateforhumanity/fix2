## üî¢ Next Step: Apply for EFIN

### What is an EFIN?
**Electronic Filing Identification Number** - Required to electronically file tax returns with the IRS.

### When to Apply:
**Wait minimum 72 hours** after workbook completion (after [specific date])

### How to Apply:

1. **Go to IRS e-Services**
   - Website: https://www.irs.gov/e-file-providers/e-services-online-tools-for-tax-professionals
   - Or: https://la.www4.irs.gov/e-services/

2. **Create Account or Login**
   - Use your SIDN: S28011182
   - Organization information
   - Contact details

3. **Complete EFIN Application**
   - Form 8633 (online)
   - Organization information
   - Responsible official information
   - Site location details
   - Background check authorization

4. **Submit Application**
   - Review all information
   - Submit electronically
   - Save confirmation number

5. **Wait for Approval**
   - Processing time: 45-60 days
   - May require additional documentation
   - Background check conducted
   - Approval notification via email

### Required Information for EFIN Application:
- SIDN: S28011182
- Organization legal name
- EIN (Employer Identification Number)
- Physical address
- Responsible official name and SSN
- Contact information
- Bank account information (for direct deposit)

## üìÖ Tax Season Operations

### Tax Season Timeline:
**January - April 15** (typically)

### Operating Hours (Recommended):
- **Weekdays:** 5:00 PM - 8:00 PM
- **Saturdays:** 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
- **Sundays:** Closed (or by appointment)

### Appointment System:
**Option 1:** Walk-in (first come, first served)  
**Option 2:** Appointment only (recommended)  
**Option 3:** Hybrid (appointments + limited walk-ins)

**Scheduling Tools:**
- Google Calendar
- Calendly
- VITA site scheduling software
- Phone appointments

### Client Flow:
1. **Intake** (15-20 min)
   - Complete Form 13614-C
   - Gather documents
   - Verify eligibility

2. **Preparation** (30-60 min)
   - Interview client
   - Enter information
   - Calculate return
   - Review with client

3. **Quality Review** (10-15 min)
   - Second set of eyes
   - Check accuracy
   - Verify compliance

4. **E-filing** (5-10 min)
   - Client signs forms
   - E-file return
   - Provide confirmation

**Total Time:** 60-105 minutes per client

## üéØ VITA Eligibility

### Who Can Use VITA Services?

**Income Limit:** Generally $64,000 or less (2024 limit, check annually)

**Eligible Clients:**
- Low to moderate income individuals
- Persons with disabilities
- Limited English speakers
- Elderly taxpayers
- Military families (special provisions)

**Services Provided:**
- Federal tax returns (Form 1040)
- State tax returns (Indiana)
- Basic returns (W-2, standard deduction)
- Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC)
- Child Tax Credit (CTC)
- Education credits
- Simple itemized deductions

**NOT Provided:**
- Business returns (Schedule C - complex)
- Rental property (Schedule E - complex)
- Stock sales (Schedule D - complex)
- Foreign income
- Amended returns (usually)
