## Getting Started

### System Requirements

**Supported Browsers:**

- Chrome 90+ (recommended)
- Firefox 88+
- Safari 14+
- Edge 90+

**Devices:**

- Desktop/Laptop
- Tablet
- Smartphone

**Internet:**

- Minimum 5 Mbps for video streaming
- 10+ Mbps recommended

## Creating an Account

### Step 1: Visit the Website

Go to: [https://www.elevateconnectsdirectory.org](https://www.elevateconnectsdirectory.org)

### Step 2: Click "Sign Up"

Located in the top right corner of the homepage.

### Step 3: Fill Out Registration Form

**Required Information:**

- Full Name
- Email Address
- Password (minimum 8 characters)
- Phone Number
- Date of Birth

**Optional Information:**

- Address
- Employment Status
- Education Level

### Step 4: Verify Email

1. Check your email inbox
2. Click verification link
3. Return to website
4. Log in with your credentials

### Step 5: Complete Profile

1. Upload profile photo (optional)
2. Add bio (optional)
3. Set learning preferences
4. Choose notification settings

## Browsing Courses

### View All Courses

1. Click "Programs" in navigation menu
2. Browse available courses
3. Use filters to narrow search:
   - Category
   - Duration
   - Level (Beginner/Intermediate/Advanced)
   - Price

### Course Information

Each course page includes:

- **Description:** What you'll learn
- **Duration:** Total hours
- **Prerequisites:** Required knowledge
- **Instructor:** Who teaches the course
- **Syllabus:** Lesson breakdown
- **Reviews:** Student feedback
- **Certificate:** What you'll earn

---

## Enrolling in Courses

### Free Courses (WIOA Funded)

1. Click "Enroll Now" on course page
2. Complete eligibility form
3. Upload required documents:
   - ID or driver's license
   - Proof of residency
   - Income verification (if applicable)
4. Wait for approval (1-3 business days)
5. Receive enrollment confirmation

### Paid Courses

1. Click "Enroll Now"
2. Review course details
3. Click "Proceed to Payment"
4. Enter payment information
5. Complete purchase
6. Access course immediately

## Taking Courses

### Accessing Your Courses

1. Log in to your account
2. Click "My Courses" in dashboard
3. Select course to continue

### Course Structure

**Modules:** Organized by topic  
**Lessons:** Individual learning units  
**Videos:** Instructional content  
**Readings:** Supplementary materials  
**Quizzes:** Knowledge checks  
**Assignments:** Practical exercises

### Watching Videos

**Controls:**

- Play/Pause
- Volume
- Playback speed (0.5x - 2x)
- Fullscreen
- Captions/Subtitles

**Tips:**

- Take notes while watching
- Pause to review concepts
- Rewatch difficult sections
- Use captions for clarity

### Taking Quizzes

**Types of Questions:**

- Multiple choice
- True/False
- Short answer
- Matching

**Quiz Rules:**

- Passing score: 80%
- Unlimited attempts
- Immediate feedback
- Explanations provided

### Submitting Assignments

1. Read assignment instructions
2. Complete required work
3. Upload files (if applicable)
4. Click "Submit"
5. Wait for instructor feedback

## Earning Certificates

### Certificate Requirements

**To earn a certificate, you must:**

1. Complete all lessons
2. Pass all quizzes (80%+)
3. Submit all assignments
4. Complete final assessment
5. Meet attendance requirements (if applicable)

### Downloading Certificates

1. Go to Dashboard > Certificates
2. Find completed course
3. Click "Download Certificate"
4. Save PDF to your device

### Sharing Certificates

**Options:**

- Download PDF
- Share on LinkedIn
- Share on Facebook
- Email to employer
- Print physical copy

### Certificate Verification

Each certificate includes:

- Unique verification code
- QR code for instant verification
- Issue date
- Expiration date (if applicable)

**Employers can verify at:**  
[https://www.elevateconnectsdirectory.org/verify](https://www.elevateconnectsdirectory.org/verify)

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

**Solutions:**

1. Check email and password
2. Click "Forgot Password" to reset
3. Clear browser cache and cookies
4. Try different browser
5. Contact support

### Video Won't Play

**Solutions:**

1. Check internet connection
2. Refresh the page
3. Clear browser cache
4. Update browser to latest version
5. Try different browser
6. Disable browser extensions

### Quiz Not Submitting

**Solutions:**

1. Check all questions answered
2. Refresh the page
3. Try submitting again
4. Contact instructor

### Certificate Not Downloading

**Solutions:**

1. Check popup blocker settings
2. Try different browser
3. Right-click and "Save As"
4. Contact support

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is the platform really free?**  
A: Yes! Most courses are 100% free through WIOA funding. No hidden fees.

**Q: How long do I have to complete a course?**  
A: Most courses have flexible deadlines. Check individual course pages for details.

**Q: Can I take multiple courses at once?**  
A: Yes! You can enroll in as many courses as you like.

**Q: Are certificates recognized by employers?**  
A: Yes! Our certificates are industry-recognized and include verification codes.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What if I don't have a computer?**  
A: You can access courses on any device with internet - smartphone, tablet, or library computer.

**Q: Do I need to download any software?**  
A: No! Everything works in your web browser.

**Q: Can I download videos to watch offline?**  
A: Currently, videos must be streamed online. Offline access coming soon!

**Q: What if my internet is slow?**  
A: Videos automatically adjust quality based on your connection speed.

### Course Questions

**Q: What if I fail a quiz?**  
A: You can retake quizzes unlimited times. Review the material and try again!

**Q: Can I get help from an instructor?**  
A: Yes! Use the "Ask Instructor" button or join live office hours.

**Q: What if I need to pause my course?**  
A: Contact your instructor or support team to discuss options.

**Q: Can I get college credit for courses?**  
A: Some courses offer college credit. Check individual course pages.

## Tips for Success

### Study Tips

1. **Set a Schedule:** Dedicate specific times for learning
2. **Take Notes:** Write down key concepts
3. **Practice Regularly:** Apply what you learn
4. **Ask Questions:** Don't hesitate to reach out
5. **Join Study Groups:** Learn with peers
6. **Stay Motivated:** Remember your goals

### Time Management

- Break lessons into smaller chunks
- Use the Pomodoro Technique (25 min study, 5 min break)
- Set daily learning goals
- Track your progress
- Celebrate milestones

### Technical Tips

- Use headphones for better audio
- Find a quiet study space
- Keep your device charged
- Bookmark important pages
- Enable notifications for deadlines
