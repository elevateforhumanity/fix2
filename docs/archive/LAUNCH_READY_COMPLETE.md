### 3. **Advisor Operating Rules** ‚úÖ

**File:** `/docs/ADVISOR_OPERATING_RULES.md`

**Rules Defined:**

1. **When to Move Forward:**
   - Eligibility verified
   - Funding confirmed or self-pay approved
   - All required documents submitted
   - Student understands program requirements

2. **When Payments Are Allowed:**
   - NEVER before advisor approval
   - Only after funding confirmation or written self-pay agreement
   - Only through invoicing (no instant checkout)
   - Only for approved programs

3. **When Partner Links Are Released:**
   - After enrollment is confirmed
   - After payment/funding is secured
   - After student orientation completed
   - With clear expectations set

4. **When to Decline or Defer:**
   - Eligibility not met
   - Funding not available and self-pay not feasible
   - Student not ready (needs prerequisites)
   - Program not appropriate for student goals

5. **Documentation Requirements:**
   - All advisor interactions logged
   - All funding decisions documented
   - All enrollment approvals recorded
   - All student communications tracked
