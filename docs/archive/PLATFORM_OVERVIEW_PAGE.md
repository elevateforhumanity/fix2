## Section 1: What It Is

### Headline

**More Than an LMS. A Complete Workforce Operating System.**

### Body Copy

Elevate for Humanity is a multi-tenant platform designed specifically for workforce training providers, employers, workforce boards, and agencies managing funded programs.

Unlike generic learning management systems, our platform handles the full lifecycle of workforce development:

- **Program management** - Create and manage training programs with apprenticeship support
- **Enrollment & tracking** - Enroll learners, track progress, and manage completions
- **Funding compatibility** - Built to align with WIOA, WRG, JRI, and employer-paid models
- **Compliance reporting** - Generate board-ready reports for attendance, outcomes, and credentials
- **Secure access** - Role-based permissions and organization-level data isolation

## Section 8: Frequently Asked Questions

### Is this a generic LMS?

No. This is a workforce operating system built specifically for funded training programs, apprenticeships, and compliance reporting. Generic LMS platforms don't handle workforce funding workflows or multi-tenant organization management.

### Can we customize it for our organization?

Yes. White-label licensing includes custom branding, domain configuration, and organizational settings. We can also discuss custom integrations for specific needs.

### What about data security?

All data is encrypted, isolated by organization, and protected with role-based access controls. We follow FERPA guidelines for educational data and maintain regular security audits.

### How long does deployment take?

Standard deployments take 2-4 weeks from contract signing to go-live. White-label deployments may take 4-6 weeks depending on customization requirements.

### Do you support WIOA reporting?

Yes. The platform includes reporting views designed to align with WIOA performance measures, including enrollment tracking, completion rates, credential attainment, and employment outcomes.

### Can we export our data?

Yes. You retain ownership of all data entered into the platform. Data export is available at any time in standard formats (CSV, JSON).
