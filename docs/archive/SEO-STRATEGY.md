## Competitor Analysis

### H&R Block Strategy

- **Their Strength**: Brand recognition, physical locations, tax pros
- **Our Advantage**: Lower cost, faster service, same-day refund advance, no appointment needed
- **Attack Angle**: "Why pay $200-$400 at H&R Block? Get the same service for less."

### TurboTax Strategy

- **Their Strength**: DIY online platform, marketing budget
- **Our Advantage**: Human tax preparers, in-person help, refund advance, complex returns
- **Attack Angle**: "Stuck on TurboTax? Get expert help in person. Same day."

### Jackson Hewitt Strategy

- **Their Strength**: Refund advance, Walmart locations
- **Our Advantage**: Higher advance amounts, faster approval, better service
- **Attack Angle**: "Get up to $6,000 refund advance. More than Jackson Hewitt."

### Liberty Tax Strategy

- **Their Strength**: Refund advance, local presence
- **Our Advantage**: No hidden fees, transparent pricing, better customer service
- **Attack Angle**: "No hidden fees. No surprises. Just your refund, fast."
