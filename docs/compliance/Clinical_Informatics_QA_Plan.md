## 1. Purpose

This Clinical Informatics Quality Assurance (QA) Plan establishes a structured framework for monitoring, evaluating, and continuously improving the Learning Management System (LMS), data integrity, student privacy, instructional quality, and accreditation compliance.

The QA Plan ensures that Elevate for Humanity maintains high standards in educational delivery, data governance, FERPA compliance, workforce alignment, and clinical training documentation through systematic review and continuous improvement.

## 5. QA Monitoring Areas

QA reviews will focus on the following areas:

### 5.1 LMS Access and Security

**Monitoring Activities:**
- Role-based access control verification
- User permission audits
- Inactive account reviews
- Password policy compliance
- Multi-factor authentication adoption
- Session timeout configuration

**Metrics:**

- Inactive accounts (>90 days)
- Failed login attempts
- Password policy violations
- MFA adoption rate

### 5.2 Audit Logs and System Activity

## 8. Corrective Action & Improvement

When issues are identified, the following process is followed:

### Step 1: Document the Issue

- **Description:** Clear description of the problem
- **Evidence:** Supporting data and documentation
- **Impact:** Assessment of severity and scope
- **Root Cause:** Analysis of underlying cause

### Step 2: Assess Risk Level

**Critical (Red):**
- FERPA violation
- Data breach
- System outage
- Accreditation risk

**High (Orange):**
- Compliance gap
- Data integrity issue
- Security vulnerability
- Significant process failure

**Medium (Yellow):**
- Process inefficiency
- Training gap
- Minor data discrepancy
- Usability issue

**Low (Green):**
- Enhancement opportunity
- Minor improvement
- Optimization suggestion

### Step 3: Develop Corrective Action Plan

- **Actions:** Specific steps to resolve issue
- **Responsible Party:** Who will implement
- **Timeline:** Deadline for completion
- **Resources:** What is needed
- **Success Criteria:** How to measure resolution

### Step 4: Assign Responsibility

- Assign to appropriate staff member
- Provide necessary authority and resources
- Set clear expectations and deadlines
- Establish checkpoints for progress

### Step 5: Monitor Implementation

- Track progress against timeline
- Provide support as needed
- Remove obstacles
- Adjust plan if necessary

### Step 6: Verify Resolution

- Test that issue is resolved
- Verify success criteria met
- Document resolution
- Close issue in tracking system

### Step 7: Prevent Recurrence

- Update policies and procedures
- Provide additional training
- Implement preventive controls
- Share lessons learned

## 10. Continuous Quality Improvement (CQI)

QA findings are used to drive continuous improvement through:

### Process Improvement

- **Identify inefficiencies** in current processes
- **Streamline workflows** to reduce errors
- **Automate repetitive tasks** where possible
- **Standardize procedures** for consistency

### Policy Updates

- **Review policies** based on findings
- **Update procedures** to reflect best practices
- **Clarify ambiguities** that cause confusion
- **Align with regulations** and standards

### LMS Enhancement

- **Optimize configuration** for usability
- **Implement new features** to meet needs
- **Improve security controls** as needed
- **Enhance reporting capabilities**

### Training Improvement

- **Update training content** based on gaps
- **Enhance delivery methods** for effectiveness
- **Provide targeted training** for specific issues
- **Measure training impact** on performance

### Data Governance

- **Strengthen data quality controls**
- **Improve data validation** processes
- **Enhance data security** measures
- **Optimize data architecture**

### Workforce Alignment

- **Align programs** with workforce needs
- **Improve outcome tracking** and reporting
- **Strengthen partner relationships**
- **Enhance student support** for employment

### CQI Cycle

1. **Plan:** Identify improvement opportunity
2. **Do:** Implement improvement on small scale
3. **Study:** Analyze results and impact
4. **Act:** Adopt, adapt, or abandon improvement
