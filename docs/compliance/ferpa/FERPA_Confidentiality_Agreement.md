# FERPA Confidentiality Agreement

**Elevate for Humanity Career & Training Institute**

---

## Purpose

This FERPA Confidentiality Agreement is required for all faculty, staff, administrators, contractors, and LMS users who have access to student education records. The purpose of this agreement is to ensure compliance with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) and to protect the confidentiality and security of student education records.

---

## Confidentiality Obligations

I understand that as an authorized user of student education records, I must:

- ☐ Access student records **only as necessary** to perform my assigned duties
- ☐ **Not disclose** student information to unauthorized individuals
- ☐ **Not discuss** student information in public or unsecured environments
- ☐ Use **only approved institutional systems** (LMS, email, databases)
- ☐ **Secure** any downloaded or printed student records
- ☐ Follow **all institutional FERPA policies and procedures**
- ☐ Complete **annual FERPA training** and maintain certification
- ☐ **Report** suspected violations immediately

---

## Prohibited Actions

The following actions are **strictly prohibited**:

### Unauthorized Disclosure
- ❌ Sharing grades or records with unauthorized persons
- ❌ Discussing student information in public areas (hallways, cafeterias, social media)
- ❌ Posting student information publicly or on social media
- ❌ Forwarding student emails without consent
- ❌ Sharing screenshots of student records

### Improper Access
- ❌ Accessing records without a legitimate educational interest
- ❌ Looking up records of friends, family, or acquaintances without job-related need
- ❌ Sharing login credentials with others
- ❌ Allowing others to use my account

### Insecure Practices
- ❌ Using personal email or devices to store student records
- ❌ Leaving student records unattended or unsecured
- ❌ Failing to log out of systems
- ❌ Displaying student information on screens visible to others
- ❌ Disposing of records without secure destruction (shredding)

### Public Posting
- ❌ Posting grades on classroom walls (even with ID numbers)
- ❌ Reading grades aloud in class
- ❌ Returning graded work in public view
- ❌ Using public spreadsheets or documents for grades

---

## Understanding of FERPA Rights

I understand that students have the right to:

1. **Inspect and review** their education records within 45 days of request
2. **Request amendment** of inaccurate or misleading records
3. **Consent to disclosure** of personally identifiable information
4. **File complaints** with the U.S. Department of Education regarding violations

---

## Understanding of Disclosure Rules

I understand that:

- **Written consent** is required for most third-party disclosures
- **Directory information** may be disclosed only if student has not opted out
- **FERPA exceptions** allow disclosure without consent in specific circumstances:
  - School officials with legitimate educational interest
  - Transfer to another educational institution
  - Financial aid determination
  - Accrediting organizations
  - Compliance with judicial order or subpoena
  - Health and safety emergencies

---

## LMS-Specific Responsibilities

As an LMS user, I agree to:

- ☐ Use **secure authentication** (strong passwords, MFA if available)
- ☐ **Log out** when finished or when leaving workstation
- ☐ Access LMS only through **secure networks** (not public Wi-Fi for sensitive data)
- ☐ Use **LMS gradebook** for posting grades (not public methods)
- ☐ Respect **role-based access** limitations
- ☐ **Not share** student submissions or work without consent
- ☐ **Report** technical issues that may compromise security

---

## Workforce Development Responsibilities

If I work with workforce development partners, I understand:

- ☐ **Data sharing agreements** must be in place before sharing student data
- ☐ **Student consent** required for individual-level data sharing
- ☐ **Aggregate data** must be de-identified
- ☐ Partners must be **trained on FERPA** requirements
- ☐ Partner compliance must be **monitored and audited**

---

## Violation Consequences

I understand that violations of FERPA may result in:

### For Me:
- Verbal or written warning
- Mandatory retraining
- Suspension of access privileges
- Termination of employment
- Legal action if warranted

### For the Institution:
- Loss of federal funding
- Accreditation sanctions
- Legal liability
- Reputational damage

---

## Reporting Obligations

I agree to report suspected FERPA violations immediately to:

- **FERPA Officer:** ferpa@elevateforhumanity.org
- **My Supervisor**
- **Compliance Department**

I understand that:
- Reporting is **mandatory**, not optional
- I will **not be retaliated against** for good-faith reporting
- Failure to report may result in disciplinary action
- I should **preserve evidence** and cooperate with investigations
