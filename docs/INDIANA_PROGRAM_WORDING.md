# Indiana-Only Program Wording

## Official Program Description

Use this exact wording anywhere you describe the Barber Apprenticeship program:

---

**Indiana Registered Apprenticeship – Barber Program**

Sponsored by Elevate for Humanity and registered with the U.S. Department of Labor (RAPIDS).

This is an Earn & Learn pathway combining paid on-the-job training with classroom instruction.

Tuition is fully covered through workforce funding for eligible participants.

---

## Key Messaging Points

### For Students
- **No cost to you** - WIOA-funded through WorkOne
- **Earn while you learn** - Paid on-the-job training at assigned barbershop
- **DOL Registered** - Official U.S. Department of Labor apprenticeship
- **Indiana State Board approved** - Leads to Indiana barber licensure
- **2,000 hours total** - 300 classroom + 1,700 on-the-job

### For Employers
- **DOL Registered Apprenticeship Sponsor** - Elevate for Humanity
- **RAPIDS certified** - Official federal apprenticeship program
- **Indiana DWD compliant** - Meets all state requirements
- **Hybrid delivery** - Milady RISE online + shop training
- **No employer cost** - WIOA covers all training expenses

### For WorkOne/Workforce Partners
- **WIOA-eligible program** - Approved for workforce funding
- **RAPIDS registered** - Program #: [pending/add when available]
- **Indiana PLA compliant** - Meets Professional Licensing Agency standards
- **ETPL listed** - Eligible Training Provider List approved
- **Completion pathway** - Leads to Indiana State Board exam eligibility

## FAQ Responses

**Q: Is this a real apprenticeship?**
A: Yes. This is a U.S. Department of Labor Registered Apprenticeship
program (RAPIDS). It meets all federal and Indiana state requirements.

**Q: Do I have to pay?**
A: No. This program is WIOA-funded through WorkOne Indiana. Eligible
students pay $0 for tuition, materials, and instruction.

**Q: Will I get paid?**
A: Yes. During your on-the-job training hours at your assigned
barbershop, you will be paid by the shop owner.

**Q: Is this available in other states?**
A: No. This is an Indiana-only program registered with Indiana's
Professional Licensing Agency.

**Q: What happens after I complete the program?**
A: You will be eligible to sit for the Indiana State Board Barber
Exam. Upon passing, you receive your Indiana barber license.
