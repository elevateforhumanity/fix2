## 1. Welcome!

Welcome to Elevate for Humanity! This guide will help you navigate your learning journey.

## 2. Getting Started

### 2.1 First Login

1. Check your email for invitation
2. Click activation link
3. Set your password
4. Complete your profile

### 2.2 Your Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:
- **My Courses**: Enrolled courses
- **Progress**: Completion status
- **Upcoming**: Deadlines and live sessions
- **Achievements**: Certificates and badges

## 3. Taking Courses

### 3.1 Course Navigation

- **Modules**: Major topics in order
- **Lessons**: Individual learning units
- **Resources**: Downloads and links
- **Assessments**: Quizzes and assignments

### 3.2 Completing Lessons

1. Click on a lesson
2. Watch videos, read content
3. Complete activities
4. Mark as complete
5. Move to next lesson

### 3.3 Offline Learning

- Download course content
- Learn without internet
- Progress syncs when online

## 4. Assessments

### 4.1 Taking Quizzes

1. Navigate to quiz
2. Read instructions
3. Answer questions
4. Submit for grading
5. View results immediately

### 4.2 Submitting Assignments

1. Download assignment instructions
2. Complete your work
3. Upload your submission
4. Wait for instructor feedback

### 4.3 Proctored Exams

1. Schedule exam time
2. Complete system check
3. Enable webcam/screen share
4. Take exam
5. Submit when complete

## 5. Live Sessions

### 5.1 Joining Live Classes

1. Check schedule on dashboard
2. Click "Join Session" at class time
3. Enable camera/microphone
4. Participate in class

### 5.2 Recordings

- Access past recordings
- Review missed classes
- Study for exams

## 10. Tips for Success

1. **Set Goals**: Define what you want to achieve
2. **Stay Organized**: Use calendar and reminders
3. **Engage**: Participate in discussions

**Q: Can I take courses offline?**  
A: Yes, download content for offline access

**Q: How long do I have to complete a course?**  
A: Check course details for specific deadlines

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: Contact your program administrator

**Q: How do I contact my instructor?**  
A: Use the messaging feature in your course
