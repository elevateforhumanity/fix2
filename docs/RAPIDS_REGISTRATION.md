# RAPIDS Program Registration

## What is RAPIDS?

RAPIDS (Registered Apprenticeship Partners Information Data System) is the U.S. Department of Labor's national database for Registered Apprenticeship programs.

## How to Get Your RAPIDS Program Number

### Option 1: Register a New Program (If Not Yet Registered)

**Website:** [https://www.apprenticeship.gov/employers/registered-apprenticeship-program](https://www.apprenticeship.gov/employers/registered-apprenticeship-program)

**Steps:**
1. Visit the Apprenticeship.gov website
2. Click "Become a Sponsor"
3. Contact your State Apprenticeship Agency (SAA) or Office of Apprenticeship (OA)
4. Submit program standards for approval
5. Receive RAPIDS program number upon approval

**For Indiana:**

## What You Need to Register (If Not Yet Registered)

### Required Documents:
1. **Program Standards** - Outline of training curriculum
2. **Work Process Schedule** - Breakdown of OJT hours by skill area
3. **Related Technical Instruction (RTI) Plan** - Classroom instruction details
4. **Wage Schedule** - Progressive wage increases
5. **Sponsor Information** - EIN, contact info, business details

### For Barber Apprenticeship:
- **Occupation Code:** 39-5011.00 (Barbers)
- **Total Hours:** 2,000 (300 RTI + 1,700 OJT)
- **State:** Indiana
- **Sponsor:** Elevate for Humanity

## Timeline

- **New Registration:** 30-90 days for approval
- **Lookup Existing:** Immediate (if already registered)
- **Verification:** 1-2 business days via phone/email

## Verification for Partners

**WorkOne/WIOA Partners can verify your program:**
1. Visit: [https://www.apprenticeship.gov/apprenticeship-finder](https://www.apprenticeship.gov/apprenticeship-finder)
2. Search: Sponsor name or program number
3. Confirm: Active status, Indiana location, Barber occupation

## Important Notes

### If You're Already Registered:
- You should have received a RAPIDS number when approved
- Check your approval letter from Indiana DWD
- Check your RAPIDS sponsor account

### If You're Not Yet Registered:
- You MUST register before operating as a sponsor
- Operating without registration is non-compliant
- Registration is free but requires approval

### Compliance Requirement:
- All DOL Registered Apprenticeships must be in RAPIDS
- Your program number is public information
- Partners (WorkOne, employers) will verify your number
