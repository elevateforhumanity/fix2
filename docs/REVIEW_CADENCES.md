# Review Cadences - Monitoring & Oversight

## Overview
This document defines review schedules, responsible parties, and service level agreements (SLAs) for all platform features and operations.

---

## 1. APPLICATIONS

**Frequency:** Daily (Monday-Friday, 9 AM EST)  
**Responsible:** Admissions Team (admissions@elevateforhumanity.org)  
**Action:** Review new applications, approve/deny, contact applicants  
**SLA:** 48 hours response time from submission  
**Metrics Tracked:**
- Applications received
- Applications approved/denied
- Average response time
- Conversion rate to enrollment

**Escalation:** Applications older than 48 hours escalate to Admissions Director

---

## 2. ENROLLMENTS

**Frequency:** Weekly (Mondays, 10 AM EST)  
**Responsible:** Program Directors  
**Action:** Audit enrollments, verify funding, confirm student start dates  
**SLA:** 7 days for funding verification  
**Metrics Tracked:**
- New enrollments
- Funding source verification status
- Enrollment completion rate
- Drop-out rate

**Escalation:** Funding issues escalate to Financial Aid Office

---

## 3. STUDENT PROGRESS

**Frequency:** Bi-weekly (1st and 15th of month, 2 PM EST)  
**Responsible:** Instructors and Academic Advisors  
**Action:** Review progress, identify at-risk students, provide interventions  
**SLA:** Contact at-risk students within 3 days of identification  
**Metrics Tracked:**
- Lesson completion rates
- Assessment scores
- Attendance percentages
- Time-to-completion

**Escalation:** Students below 67% completion rate escalate to Student Success Team

---

## 4. CERTIFICATES

**Frequency:** Monthly (1st of month, 10 AM EST)  
**Responsible:** Registrar (registrar@elevateforhumanity.org)  
**Action:** Audit issued certificates, verify completion requirements, process requests  
**SLA:** Issue within 5 business days of program completion  
**Metrics Tracked:**
- Certificates issued
- Verification requests
- Revocations (if any)
- Processing time

**Escalation:** Delayed certificates escalate to Registrar after 5 days

---

## 5. FORUM MODERATION

**Frequency:** Daily (Monday-Friday, 2 PM EST)  
**Responsible:** Community Manager (community@elevateforhumanity.org)  
**Action:** Review flagged posts, moderate content, address violations  
**SLA:** 24 hours for flagged content review  
**Metrics Tracked:**
- Posts flagged
- Posts removed
- User warnings/suspensions
- Response time

**Escalation:** Serious violations (threats, harassment) escalate immediately to Director

---

## 6. AI TUTOR INTERACTIONS

**Frequency:** Weekly (Fridays, 3 PM EST)  
**Responsible:** AI Oversight Committee  
**Action:** Review AI responses, identify quality issues, update training data  
**SLA:** 7 days for quality review  
**Metrics Tracked:**
- Total interactions
- User satisfaction ratings
- Flagged responses
- Error rate

**Escalation:** Inappropriate AI responses escalate to Technical Team immediately

---

## 7. CONTACT MESSAGES

**Frequency:** Daily (Monday-Friday, 9 AM and 2 PM EST)  
**Responsible:** Front Desk (info@elevateforhumanity.org)  
**Action:** Respond to inquiries, route to appropriate departments  
**SLA:** 24 hours response time  
**Metrics Tracked:**
- Messages received
- Response time
- Resolution rate
- Follow-up required

**Escalation:** Urgent inquiries escalate to Department Heads

---

## 8. SAM.GOV OPPORTUNITIES

**Frequency:** Daily (6 AM EST via automated cron job)  
**Responsible:** Grants Team (grants@elevateforhumanity.org)  
**Action:** Review new opportunities, flag relevant grants, assess eligibility  
**SLA:** 48 hours for relevance assessment  
**Metrics Tracked:**
- New opportunities synced
- Opportunities flagged as relevant
- Applications submitted
- Awards received

**Escalation:** High-value opportunities (>$100K) escalate to Executive Director

---

## 9. BLOG CONTENT

**Frequency:** Monthly (1st of month, 11 AM EST)  
**Responsible:** Content Manager (content@elevateforhumanity.org)  
**Action:** Review analytics, plan content calendar, update posts  
**SLA:** Publish 3-5 posts per month  
**Metrics Tracked:**
- Page views
- Engagement rate
- SEO performance
- Social shares

**Escalation:** Declining metrics escalate to Marketing Director

---

## 10. POLICY REVIEW

**Frequency:** Quarterly (January 1, April 1, July 1, October 1)  
**Responsible:** Compliance Officer (compliance@elevateforhumanity.org)  
**Action:** Review all policies, update as needed, ensure regulatory compliance  
**SLA:** 30 days for policy updates  
**Metrics Tracked:**
- Policies reviewed
- Policies updated
- Compliance issues identified
- Regulatory changes

**Escalation:** Regulatory violations escalate to Legal Counsel immediately

## AUDIT TRAIL REQUIREMENTS

All reviews must be documented with:
- Date and time of review
- Reviewer name
- Items reviewed
- Actions taken
- Follow-up required
- Next review date

**Documentation Location:** `/admin/audits/[feature]/` dashboards
