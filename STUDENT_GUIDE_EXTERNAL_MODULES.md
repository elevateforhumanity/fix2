## Student Guide: Completing Partner Modules

### What Are Partner Modules?

Partner modules are industry-recognized training courses from professional organizations like Milady RISE, HSI, Certiport, and others. These modules are **required parts of your Elevate course** and will appear on your final certificate as stacked credentials.

---

**Industry Recognition:**
- Certifications from nationally-recognized organizations
- Credentials employers look for
- Professional standards compliance

**Stacked Credentials:**
- All certifications appear on one Elevate certificate
- Shows comprehensive training
- Increases employability

### How to Complete Partner Modules

#### Step 1: Find Your Partner Modules

1. Log in to your Elevate dashboard
2. Go to your course
3. Look for modules labeled "External Partner Module"
4. You'll see partner name (e.g., "Milady RISE")

#### Step 2: Launch the Partner Course

1. Click on the partner module
2. Read the module description
3. Click "Launch [Partner] Course"
4. New tab opens with partner's website
5. Complete the training on their platform

**Important:** Keep your Elevate tab open!

#### Step 3: Complete the Training

**On the Partner Site:**
- Watch all videos
- Complete all quizzes
- Pass final assessment
- Download your certificate

**Time Required:**
- Varies by module (2-15 hours)
- Can be done in multiple sessions
- Partner site saves your progress

#### Step 4: Upload Your Proof

**Return to Elevate:**
1. Go back to the Elevate tab
2. Scroll to "Step 2 – Upload your certificate"
3. Click "Choose File"
4. Select your certificate (PDF or image)
5. Click upload

**What to Upload:**
- Completion certificate (PDF preferred)
- Screenshot of completion page
- Must show your name and completion date

#### Step 5: Wait for Approval

**What Happens:**
- Admin reviews your submission (usually within 24 hours)
- Status changes to "Approved" when verified
- You receive notification

**If Reset:**
- Admin may ask you to re-upload
- Check your email for instructions
- Upload corrected document

#### Step 6: Check Course Completion

**Once All Modules Approved:**
1. Return to your course page
2. Click "Check Completion"
3. System verifies all requirements
4. Course marked as complete
5. Certificate issued!

### Common Questions

#### Q: Do I have to pay for partner courses?

**A:** It depends on the partner:
- **Free:** JRI, NRF RISE Up
- **Included in tuition:** Most partners
- **Promo codes available:** Milady RISE (use code: efhcti-rise295)

Your program coordinator will tell you if there are any costs.

#### Q: How long do I have to complete partner modules?

**A:** Partner modules must be completed before you can finish your Elevate course. Plan to complete them alongside your regular lessons.

#### Q: What if I lose my certificate?

**A:** Most partners keep certificates in your account:
1. Log back into partner site
2. Go to "My Certificates" or similar
3. Download again
4. Re-upload to Elevate

#### Q: Can I complete partner modules in any order?

**A:** Yes! Complete them in whatever order works for you. All must be approved before course completion.

#### Q: What if the partner site isn't working?

**A:** Contact Elevate support:
- Email: support@elevateforhumanity.org
- Phone: 317-314-3757
- We'll help you troubleshoot or contact the partner

#### Q: Do partner certifications expire?

**A:** Some do:
- **HSI (CPR/First Aid):** 2 years
- **CareerSafe (OSHA):** No expiration
- **Certiport:** No expiration
- **Milady RISE:** No expiration

Check your certificate for expiration dates.

#### Q: What if my submission is rejected?

**A:** Admin will explain why:
- Wrong course uploaded
- Incomplete certificate
- Unreadable image
- Missing information

Fix the issue and re-upload.

#### Q: Can I get help with partner courses?

**A:** Yes!
- **Technical issues:** Contact partner support
- **Content questions:** Contact partner support
- **Upload issues:** Contact Elevate support
- **General questions:** Contact your program coordinator

### Partner-Specific Tips

#### Milady RISE

**Website:** miladytraining.com

**Tips:**
- Use promo code: efhcti-rise295
- Course takes 3-4 hours
- Can pause and resume
- Certificate downloads as PDF

**Common Issues:**
- Make sure to complete ALL modules
- Don't upload enrollment confirmation
- Upload the actual completion certificate

#### HSI (Health & Safety Institute)

**Website:** hsi.com

**Tips:**
- CPR/First Aid course takes 4 hours
- Includes video demonstrations
- Must pass skills assessment
- Certificate valid for 2 years

**Common Issues:**
- Upload the card, not the class registration
- Make sure completion date is visible
- Include card number in upload

#### CareerSafe (OSHA)

**Website:** careersafeonline.com

**Tips:**
- OSHA 10 takes 10 hours minimum
- Must complete in 6 months
- Can do 1-2 hours at a time
- Certificate includes DOL number

**Common Issues:**
- Don't upload partial completion
- Make sure all 10 hours are done
- Upload the official DOL card

#### Certiport

**Website:** certiport.com

**Tips:**
- Practice tests available
- Actual exam is timed
- Must pass to get certificate
- Can retake if needed

**Common Issues:**
- Don't upload practice test results
- Upload actual certification, not exam score
- Make sure it says "Pass"

#### NDS (National Drug Screening)

**Website:** nationaldrugscreening.com

**Tips:**
- Course takes 2-4 hours
- All online, no in-person
- Certificate downloads immediately
- Valid for employment screening

**Common Issues:**
- Upload completion certificate, not enrollment
- Make sure certificate number is visible

### Troubleshooting

#### Problem: Can't Access Partner Site

**Solutions:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try different browser
3. Clear browser cache
4. Contact partner support
5. Contact Elevate if still stuck

#### Problem: Forgot Partner Login

**Solutions:**
1. Use "Forgot Password" on partner site
2. Check email for login info
3. Contact partner support
4. Elevate can help coordinate

#### Problem: Upload Fails

**Solutions:**
1. Check file size (under 10MB)
2. Try different file format (PDF or JPG)
3. Compress large images
4. Try different browser
5. Contact Elevate support

#### Problem: Certificate Not Downloading

**Solutions:**
1. Check browser's download folder
2. Try different browser
3. Right-click and "Save As"
4. Take screenshot as backup
5. Contact partner support

#### Problem: Status Stuck on "Submitted"

**Solutions:**
1. Wait 24 hours for review
2. Check email for admin feedback
3. Contact Elevate if over 48 hours
4. May need to re-upload
