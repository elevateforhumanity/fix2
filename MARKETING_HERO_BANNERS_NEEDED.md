## ðŸŽ¨ GENERAL GUIDELINES FOR ALL 27 IMAGES

### Visual Style:
- **Real people** (not stock photo looking)
- **Diverse representation** (age, race, gender, ability)
- **Professional but approachable**
- **Bright, well-lit** (avoid dark/moody)
- **Action-oriented** (people doing things, not just posing)

### Technical Requirements:
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 pixels
- **DPI:** 300
- **Format:** JPG
- **File Size:** 200-600KB (optimized)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9

### Color Palette:
- **Primary:** Orange (#F97316) and Red (#DC2626)
- **Secondary:** Warm neutrals, professional blues
- **Avoid:** Dark/moody tones, overly corporate grays
- **Prefer:** Bright, welcoming, energetic colors

### Composition:
- **Rule of thirds** for subject placement
- **Negative space** for text overlay
- **Left or right alignment** (leave space for headlines)
- **Depth of field** (slight blur on background okay)

### People in Images:
- **Authentic expressions** (genuine smiles, focused concentration)
- **Diverse ages** (18-55+)
- **Various settings** (classroom, workplace, home, community)
- **Appropriate attire** (business casual to work uniforms)

### Settings:
- **Classroom environments**
- **Training facilities**
- **Professional workplaces**
- **Community spaces**
- **Home/remote learning**
- **Outdoor campus areas**
