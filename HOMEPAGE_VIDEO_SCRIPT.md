## ðŸŽ¥ Recording Tips

### Voiceover Recording

1. **Use a quiet room** - No background noise
2. **Stand while recording** - Better breath control
3. **Smile while speaking** - It comes through in your voice
4. **Record in short segments** - Easier to edit
5. **Do multiple takes** - Pick the best one
6. **Speak 20% slower** - Easier to understand
7. **Emphasize key words** - "workforce," "compliance," "built for you"

### Screen Recording

1. **Clean your desktop** - Close unnecessary apps
2. **Use 1920x1080 resolution** - Standard HD
3. **Zoom to 125%** - Makes text readable
4. **Slow mouse movements** - Easier to follow
5. **Pause between actions** - Gives viewers time to process
6. **Record in segments** - Easier to edit
7. **Use cursor highlighting** - Shows where to look

## ðŸŒŸ Pro Tips

### Make It Feel Premium

- **Professional voiceover** - Consider hiring a voice actor
- **High-quality music** - Don't use free YouTube tracks
- **Smooth animations** - No jarring transitions
- **Consistent branding** - Use your color palette
- **Captions always** - 85% watch without sound

### Make It Authentic

- **Show real platform** - Not mockups
- **Use real data** - Anonymized if needed
- **Include real use cases** - Specific examples
- **Be honest about roadmap** - "Coming soon" is okay
- **Show your passion** - Why you built this matters

### Make It Actionable

- **Clear CTA** - One primary action
- **Easy next step** - "Schedule a demo" not "Contact us"
- **Remove friction** - No forms with 20 fields
- **Follow up fast** - Respond within 1 hour
- **Deliver value** - Demo should wow them
