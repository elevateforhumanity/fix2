## SIGNS OF AI-GENERATED OR FAKE STOCK PHOTOS

### Red Flags to Check:
1. **Overly perfect lighting** - too studio-like
2. **Unnatural skin texture** - too smooth or plastic-looking
3. **Weird hands/fingers** - AI struggles with hands
4. **Generic poses** - typical stock photo poses
5. **Fake smiles** - not genuine expressions
6. **Background inconsistencies** - blurred or odd backgrounds
7. **Watermarks or stock photo feel**
8. **Too polished** - doesn't look like real workplace

### What REAL Photos Should Have:
1. **Authentic workplace settings** - actual training facilities, real equipment
2. **Natural lighting** - not overly staged
3. **Genuine expressions** - real people doing real work
4. **Proper context** - shows actual program activities
5. **Good composition** - professional but authentic
6. **Real students/instructors** - if possible, YOUR actual students
