# SkillUp Coalition vs Elevate for Humanity - Side-by-Side Comparison

## Executive Summary

**SkillUp Coalition** is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit career platform with 150+ coalition partners, 3.9M+ workers supported, and a proven track record of job placement and training referrals.

**Elevate for Humanity** is a workforce training provider with ETPL-approved programs, but positioned more as a training school than a comprehensive career platform.

## What You Have That SkillUp Doesn't

### 1. **White-Label Platform Licensing**

- You offer platform licensing
- SkillUp doesn't sell their platform
- This is your competitive advantage

### 2. **Direct Training Delivery**

- You run the training programs
- SkillUp just refers to partners
- You control quality

### 3. **ETPL-Approved Programs**

- You have official ETPL programs
- Direct WIOA/WRG funding
- SkillUp just aggregates others' programs

### 4. **Apprenticeship Sponsorship**

- You're a DOL-registered apprenticeship sponsor
- SkillUp just lists apprenticeships
- You can place students directly

### 5. **Integrated LMS**

- You have course delivery built-in
- SkillUp doesn't deliver training
- You're end-to-end

## Key Takeaway

**SkillUp is positioned as a comprehensive career platform and nonprofit coalition.**

**Elevate is positioned as a training provider with a platform.**

**To compete for LMS buyers, you need to:**

1. Show you're more than just a school
2. Demonstrate ecosystem/coalition model
3. Prove impact with detailed metrics
4. Offer career exploration, not just training
5. Build trust through nonprofit transparency

**Your advantage:** You actually deliver training AND have a licensable platform. SkillUp just aggregates. Position yourself as "SkillUp + Training Delivery + White-Label Platform."
