## ANALYTICS & OPTIMIZATION

### Track These Metrics

1. **Most Visited Pages** (top 100)
2. **Least Visited Pages** (bottom 100)
3. **Navigation Paths** (how users navigate)
4. **Search Queries** (what users search for)
5. **Dead Ends** (pages with high exit rates)
6. **Conversion Paths** (paths to Apply)

### Optimization Strategy

1. **Promote Popular Pages** (add to header/footer)
2. **Improve Unpopular Pages** (better content/links)
3. **Fix Dead Ends** (add related links)
4. **Optimize Search** (improve results)
5. **Simplify Paths** (reduce clicks to conversion)
