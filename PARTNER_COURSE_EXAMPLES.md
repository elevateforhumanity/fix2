# Partner Course Integration Examples

## Real-World Course Configurations

This document shows how partner modules integrate into actual Elevate courses to create stacked credentials.

## 1. CNA (Certified Nursing Assistant) Program

### Course Structure

**Internal Elevate Modules:**
1. Introduction to CNA
2. Patient Care Basics
3. Medical Terminology
4. Vital Signs & Measurements
5. Infection Control
6. Patient Rights & Ethics
7. Clinical Skills Lab

**External Partner Modules:**
1. **HSI - CPR, AED & First Aid** (4 hours) - Required
2. **CareerSafe - Healthcare Safety & Bloodborne Pathogens** (8 hours) - Required
3. **NDS - Drug-Free Workplace for Healthcare** (4 hours) - Required

### Total Program
- **Internal Hours:** 60 hours
- **External Hours:** 16 hours
- **Total:** 76 hours

### Credential Stack
```
Certificate of Completion
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Certified Nursing Assistant Training Program

Credentials Earned:
• Elevate CNA Training Certificate
• HSI CPR, AED & First Aid Certification
• CareerSafe Healthcare Safety Certification
• National Drug Screening Healthcare Compliance
```

### Student Journey
1. Complete internal lessons 1-7
2. Launch HSI course → Complete CPR training → Upload certificate
3. Launch CareerSafe → Complete OSHA training → Auto-synced
4. Launch NDS → Complete drug-free training → Auto-synced
5. All requirements met → Certificate issued

## Revenue Calculation

### Example: CNA Program with Partners

**Course Price:** $1,200

- Option 1: Include in course price (no additional charge)
