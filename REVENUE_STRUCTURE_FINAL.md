# ✅ REVENUE STRUCTURE - FINAL AND CORRECT

## 40/60 Split Structure

### Suboffice Receives:

- **60% of base tax preparation fee**
- **NO add-on fees**
- **NO software costs**
- **NO training fees**
- **NO support fees**

### Main Office (Elevate for Humanity) Receives:

- **40% of base tax preparation fee**
- **100% of all add-on fees:**
  - Refund advance fees
  - Prepaid card fees
  - Bank product fees
  - Upsell revenue
  - Additional services

---

## Example Calculation

```
Client pays: $300 for tax preparation
Add-on fees: $50 (refund advance fee)

Suboffice receives:
  $300 × 60% = $180

Main office receives:
  $300 × 40% = $120 (base fee)
  $50 × 100% = $50 (add-on fees)
  Total: $170
```
