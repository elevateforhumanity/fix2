## üéØ FUNDING PROGRAMS EXPLAINED

### WRG (Workforce Ready Grant)
- **What it is**: Indiana state grant program
- **Who qualifies**: Indiana residents pursuing high-demand careers
- **Benefit**: Covers training costs
- **Programs**: Medical Assistant, Barber Apprenticeship

### WIOA (Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act)
- **What it is**: Federal workforce development program
- **Who qualifies**: Low-income, unemployed, dislocated workers, veterans
- **Benefits**: Free tuition, transportation, childcare support
- **Programs**: Medical Assistant, Barber Apprenticeship

### Workforce Grants
- **What it is**: Various federal and state workforce funding
- **Who qualifies**: Varies by program
- **Benefits**: Training cost coverage
- **Programs**: HVAC, Building Maintenance, CDL

### Apprenticeship Programs
- **What it is**: Registered apprenticeship (earn while you learn)
- **Who qualifies**: Varies by program
- **Benefits**: On-the-job training + classroom instruction
- **Programs**: Barber, Building Maintenance

### JRI (Justice Reinvestment Initiative)
- **What it is**: Second chance program for returning citizens
- **Who qualifies**: Individuals with criminal justice involvement
- **Benefits**: Free training, support services, job placement
- **Programs**: Available for most programs
