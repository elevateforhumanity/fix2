## Music Recommendations

For background music, use royalty-free tracks with these characteristics:
- **Tempo**: 90-120 BPM (moderate, uplifting)
- **Mood**: Inspirational, hopeful, professional
- **Volume**: 20-30% of narration volume (subtle background)
- **Genre**: Corporate/motivational, light electronic, or acoustic

### Suggested Sources:
- YouTube Audio Library (free)
- Epidemic Sound
- Artlist (you're already using their content)
- Uppbeat
- Pixabay Music

### Recommended Tracks Style:
- Uplifting corporate background music
- Inspirational acoustic tracks
- Soft electronic/ambient for modern feel
- Avoid lyrics to keep focus on narration

---

## Production Notes

1. **Text-to-Speech Settings**:
   - Voice: Professional, warm, clear (e.g., Google Cloud TTS, Amazon Polly)
   - Speed: Normal to slightly slower for clarity
   - Pitch: Neutral to slightly lower for authority

2. **Audio Mixing**:
   - Narration: -3dB to 0dB (primary)
   - Music: -20dB to -15dB (background)
   - Fade music in/out at start/end (0.5s fade)

3. **Video Processing**:
   - Keep original video quality
   - Add audio track without re-encoding video if possible
   - Export as MP4 (H.264 video, AAC audio)

4. **File Naming**:
   - Use exact filenames listed above
   - Place site videos in: `public/videos/`
   - Place course videos in: `public/videos/courses/`
