## üé® Image Guidelines

### Hero Banners:
- **Resolution:** 2400x1350 (16:9)
- **Format:** WebP + JPEG fallback
- **Quality:** 85-90
- **File Size:** < 300KB
- **Content:** Bright, clear, action-oriented
- **Lighting:** Natural or well-lit
- **Composition:** Rule of thirds, clear subject

### Program Cards:
- **Resolution:** 1200x800 (3:2)
- **Format:** WebP + JPEG fallback
- **Quality:** 85
- **File Size:** < 200KB
- **Content:** Program-specific, engaging

### Profile Photos:
- **Resolution:** 800x800 (1:1)
- **Format:** WebP + JPEG fallback
- **Quality:** 90
- **File Size:** < 100KB
- **Content:** Professional headshot
- **Background:** Consistent, neutral
