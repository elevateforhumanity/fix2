- Meets all credential requirements
- Approved by accrediting bodies
- Compliant with WIOA/WRG standards

**2. Your Customization**
- Add your teaching methods

- Create your assessments
- Design your activities
- **Must cover all master syllabus objectives**

**3. LMS Integration**
- Import from your existing LMS
- Blackboard, Canvas, Moodle compatible
- SCORM package support
- Automatic sync with master syllabus
- Maintain your course structure

**4. Compliance Assurance**
