## üé¨ VIDEO PRODUCTION TIPS

### Lighting:
- Natural light when possible
- Three-point lighting for interviews
- Avoid harsh shadows
- Consistent color temperature

### Audio:
- Use external microphone
- Minimize background noise
- Record room tone
- Test audio levels

### Framing:
- Rule of thirds
- Headroom (not too much/little)
- Eye level for interviews
- Variety of angles

### B-Roll:
- Shoot 3x more than you need
- Get establishing shots
- Capture details
- Show process/action
- Get reactions
