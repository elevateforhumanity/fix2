## The Real Problem: You Need a BIN

### What is a BIN?
**BIN = Bank Identification Number**
- First 6 digits of card number
- Identifies the issuing bank
- Required for Visa/Mastercard network
- Only banks can issue BINs
- You can't buy a BIN on Temu/Alibaba

### Example:
```
Card Number: 4532 1234 5678 9012
             ^^^^
             This is the BIN

4532 = Visa card issued by specific bank
```

### Why You Can't Just Print Any Number:
1. **BIN must be registered** with Visa/Mastercard
2. **Bank must sponsor** your card program
3. **Processor must route** transactions
4. **Compliance required** (KYC, AML, etc.)
5. **Insurance needed** for fraud protection
