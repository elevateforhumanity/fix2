## STRATEGIC RATIONALE

### **Why This Order?**

**1. Buy Indiana First:**

- Easiest and fastest
- Immediate benefit on state bids
- Builds confidence
- No complex requirements

**2. Indiana MBE/WBE Second:**

- Still relatively easy
- State-level preference
- Complements Buy Indiana
- Opens supplier diversity opportunities

**3. WOSB/EDWOSB Third:**

- Federal certification
- Moderate complexity
- Quick approval (4-6 weeks)
- Opens federal set-asides
- Can use while waiting for 8(a)

**4. DBE/ACDBE Fourth:**

- You already have INDOT access
- Transportation contracts available
- Moderate complexity
- Good revenue opportunity

**5. 8(a) Fifth:**

- Most complex and longest process
- Start early but don't wait for it
- Use other certifications while waiting
- Biggest long-term opportunity

**6. HUBZone Last:**

- Only if address qualifies
- Moderate-complex process
- Good to have but not critical
- Can pursue after others approved
