## WHAT IS DBE/ACDBE?

**DBE (Disadvantaged Business Enterprise):**

- For transportation-related contracts (highways, bridges, transit)
- INDOT and federally-funded transportation projects
- Set-aside goals for DBE participation

**ACDBE (Airport Concession DBE):**

- For airport concession contracts
- Food, retail, services at airports
- Indianapolis International Airport opportunities

## DBE CONTRACT OPPORTUNITIES

**INDOT Projects:**

- Highway construction and maintenance
- Bridge projects
- Professional services (engineering, consulting)
- Materials and supplies
- Equipment rental
- Training and workforce development

**Goal:** INDOT has annual DBE participation goals (typically 10-15% of contract value)

**How to Find Opportunities:**

- INDOT website: in.gov/indot/doing-business-with-indot
- Bid opportunities portal
- Pre-bid meetings and networking events
- Prime contractor outreach

## TIPS FOR SUCCESS

✅ **Demonstrate Control** - Show owner manages daily operations  
✅ **Prove Independence** - Business operates independently  
✅ **Document Disadvantage** - Clear narrative of social/economic disadvantage  
✅ **Show Capability** - Highlight experience and qualifications  
✅ **Complete Application** - No missing information  
✅ **Respond Quickly** - Answer any INDOT requests promptly  
✅ **Maintain Compliance** - Keep certifications and registrations current
