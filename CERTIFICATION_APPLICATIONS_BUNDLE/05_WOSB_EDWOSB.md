## WHAT IS WOSB/EDWOSB?

**WOSB (Women-Owned Small Business):**

- 51%+ owned by women
- Access to WOSB set-aside contracts
- Sole-source contracts up to $4M (goods/services) or $7M (manufacturing)

**EDWOSB (Economically Disadvantaged WOSB):**

- WOSB + economic disadvantage
- Additional set-aside opportunities
- Personal net worth below $750,000 (excluding home and business)

**Recommendation:** Apply for both WOSB and EDWOSB

## NEXT STEPS

1. **Submit Application** (certify.sba.gov)
   - Complete online application
   - Upload all documents
   - Pay attention to detail

2. **SBA Review** (30-60 days)
   - Document verification
   - May request additional information

3. **Approval**
   - Receive WOSB/EDWOSB certification
   - Listed in SBA database
   - Eligible for set-aside contracts

4. **Annual Renewal**
   - Update financial information
   - Confirm continued eligibility

## BENEFITS

**WOSB Set-Asides:**

- Contracts reserved for women-owned businesses
- Sole-source awards up to $4M (services) or $7M (manufacturing)
- Competitive advantage on federal contracts

**EDWOSB Additional Benefits:**

- More set-aside opportunities
- Priority consideration
- Additional contract preferences

**Industries with WOSB Goals:**

- Professional services
- Education and training
- Healthcare
- Technology
- Consulting
