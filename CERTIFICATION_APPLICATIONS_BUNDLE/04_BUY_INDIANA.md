## Indiana Preference for State Contracts

**Program:** Buy Indiana Certification  
**Agency:** Indiana Department of Administration  
**Statute:** Indiana Code 5-22-15-20.5  
**Website:** in.gov/idoa/procurement  
**Phone:** (317) 232-3061

---

## WHAT IS BUY INDIANA?

**Benefit:**

- Price preference on Indiana state contracts
- Indiana businesses get advantage over out-of-state bidders
- Simple certification process

**Eligibility:**

- Business must be located in Indiana
- Must have Indiana business registration
- Must pay Indiana taxes

## BENEFITS OF BUY INDIANA

**Price Preference:**

- Indiana businesses receive preference when bids are close
- Helps win state contracts against out-of-state competitors
- Shows commitment to Indiana economy

**Eligible Contracts:**

- All Indiana state agency contracts
- State university contracts
- Local government contracts (many adopt Buy Indiana)

---

## NEXT STEPS

1. **Verify Indiana Registration**
   - Check Secretary of State registration
   - Confirm tax registrations current

2. **Submit Application**
   - Through Indiana procurement portal
   - Or with individual bid submissions

3. **Include in Bids**
   - Note Buy Indiana status on all state bids
   - Claim preference when applicable
