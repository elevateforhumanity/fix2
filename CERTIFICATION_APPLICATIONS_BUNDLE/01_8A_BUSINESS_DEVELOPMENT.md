## NEXT STEPS AFTER SUBMISSION

1. **SBA Review** (30-90 days)
   - Initial eligibility screening
   - Document verification
   - Site visit may be required

2. **Interview** (if requested)
   - Be prepared to discuss business operations
   - Explain disadvantaged status
   - Demonstrate management capability

3. **Approval** (6-9 months total)
   - Receive 8(a) certification
   - Assigned SBA Business Opportunity Specialist
   - Begin accessing 8(a) contracts

4. **Program Benefits** (9 years)
   - Sole-source contracts up to $4M (goods/services) or $7M (manufacturing)
   - Set-aside contracts reserved for 8(a) firms
   - Business development assistance
   - Mentor-protégé opportunities
   - Training and counseling
