**Portal:** certify.sba.gov

---

## WHAT IS HUBZONE?

**HUBZone Program:**

- For businesses in Historically Underutilized Business Zones
- 10% federal contracting goal for HUBZone businesses
- Sole-source contracts up to $7M (goods/services) or $10M (manufacturing)
- Price evaluation preference (10%)

**Requirements:**

- Principal office in HUBZone
- 35% of employees live in HUBZone
- 51%+ owned by U.S. citizens

## NEXT STEPS

1. **Verify HUBZone Status**
   - Check address at maps.certify.sba.gov/hubzone/map
   - If not in HUBZone, cannot apply

2. **Gather Employee Documentation**
   - Verify 35% live in HUBZone
   - Collect proof of residency

3. **Submit Application** (certify.sba.gov)
   - Complete online application
   - Upload all documents

4. **SBA Review** (60-90 days)
   - Document verification
   - Site visit (may be required)
   - Employee verification

5. **Approval**
   - Receive HUBZone certification
   - Listed in SBA database
   - Eligible for set-aside contracts

6. **Annual Recertification**
   - Verify continued compliance
   - Update employee information
   - Confirm principal office still in HUBZone

---

## BENEFITS

**HUBZone Set-Asides:**

- Contracts reserved for HUBZone businesses
- Sole-source awards up to $7M (services) or $10M (manufacturing)
- 10% price evaluation preference
- 3% federal contracting goal

**Competitive Advantages:**

- Priority on federal contracts
- Stacks with other certifications (8(a), WOSB, etc.)
- Access to HUBZone-only opportunities

---

## MAINTAINING CERTIFICATION

**Ongoing Requirements:**

- Principal office must remain in HUBZone
- 35% of employees must live in HUBZone
- Annual recertification required
- Notify SBA of any changes

**What Happens if HUBZone Designation Changes?**

- You have 1 year grace period
- Must relocate or lose certification
- Check HUBZone map regularly

---

## TIPS FOR SUCCESS

✅ **Verify Address First** - Don't apply if not in HUBZone  
✅ **Document Employees** - Maintain proof of 35% residency  
✅ **Show Operations** - Prove principal office is real  
✅ **Be Accurate** - SBA verifies everything  
✅ **Plan for Changes** - HUBZone maps update periodically  
✅ **Maintain Compliance** - Annual recertification required

## IMPORTANT NOTE

**Check HUBZone Status BEFORE Applying:**

If your address is NOT in a HUBZone, you CANNOT get this certification. Consider:

- Moving to HUBZone location
- Opening satellite office in HUBZone
- Focusing on other certifications (8(a), WOSB, DBE, MBE)
