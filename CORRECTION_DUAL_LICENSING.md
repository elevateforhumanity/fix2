# CORRECTION: Dual Licensing Model

**Date:** December 26, 2024  
**Issue:** Initial evaluation documents didn't fully explain the Independent Platform option

---

## WHAT WAS MISSING

The initial evaluation focused heavily on the **Program Holder Network** (MOU-based credential sharing) but didn't adequately explain the **Independent Platform License** (white-label without credentials).

---

## CORRECTED UNDERSTANDING

### There Are TWO Distinct Licensing Models:

#### 1. Program Holder Network (WITH Credentials)

- **Target:** Organizations WITHOUT credentials
- **Value:** Operate under master ETPL credentials via MOU
- **Includes:** Credentials + Platform + Compliance automation
- **Pricing:** $2K-$15K/month
- **Innovation:** Novel credential-sharing model
- **Approval:** Required (5-step application)

#### 2. Independent Platform (WITHOUT Credentials)

- **Target:** Organizations WITH their own credentials
- **Value:** White-label platform, full operational control
- **Includes:** Platform only (LMS, mobile app, reporting)
- **Pricing:** $2K-$10K/month
- **Innovation:** Standard white-label SaaS
- **Approval:** None (instant access)

---

## WHY THIS MATTERS

### Expands Market Opportunity

- **Program Holder Network:** 500-1,000 potential customers (small nonprofits)
- **Independent Platform:** 200-300 potential customers (existing ETPL providers)
- **Total:** 700-1,300 potential customers

### Reduces Risk

- If credential-sharing faces legal challenges, Independent Platform continues
- If compliance automation fails, Independent Platform is unaffected
- Two revenue streams, not one

### Strategic Positioning

- **Program Holder Network:** Novel, no direct competitors
- **Independent Platform:** Proven model, competes with traditional LMS
- Together: Unique positioning in market
