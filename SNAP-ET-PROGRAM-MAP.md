## 80-Hour Monthly Compliance

### How We Meet the Requirement

All programs are structured to provide **80+ hours per month** through:

1. **Scheduled Classroom/Lab Time**
   - In-person instruction
   - Hands-on practice
   - Skills demonstrations

2. **Online Learning Hours**
   - Self-paced modules
   - Video instruction
   - Assignments and assessments

3. **Work-Based Learning**
   - Apprenticeship hours (paid employment)
   - On-the-job training
   - Supervised work experience

4. **Supervised Job Search**
   - Career coaching sessions
   - Resume/interview prep
   - Employer connections

5. **Support Services**
   - Case management meetings
   - Barrier removal assistance
   - Progress check-ins
