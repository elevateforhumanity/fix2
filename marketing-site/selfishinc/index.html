About SELFISH INC

SELFISH INC is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to removing barriers to success through holistic support services. We recognize that true success requires support in multiple areas of life: mental wellness, career development, and financial literacy.

Our three operating programs work together to provide comprehensive support:

  • Selfish Inc Mental Wellness - Addressing emotional and psychological barriers
  • Elevate for Humanity Training Center - Building career skills and employment opportunities
  • RISE Forward Foundation - Developing financial literacy and economic empowerment

We also partner with Curvature Body Sculpting to provide optional physical wellness services for those seeking body contouring and self-care support.
