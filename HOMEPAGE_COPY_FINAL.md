## ABOUT ELEVATE FOR HUMANITY

Elevate For Humanity is a workforce training institute built to remove barriers, support families, and help individuals step into high-demand careers.

We provide career training, funding support, reentry programs, apprenticeships, case-manager coordination, and job placement — all in one ecosystem.

**Our mission is simple:** Empower people. Elevate communities. Change lives.
