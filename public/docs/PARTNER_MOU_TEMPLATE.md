## II. PARTNERSHIP SCOPE

### A. Elevate for Humanity Responsibilities

Elevate for Humanity agrees to:

1. **Student Recruitment & Referrals**
   - Market programs to eligible students
   - Pre-screen applicants for basic eligibility
   - Coordinate enrollment process
   - Provide student onboarding support

2. **Funding Coordination**
   - Assist with WIOA, WRG, JAG, and other funding applications
   - Coordinate with workforce boards and funding agencies
   - Process tuition payments according to agreed terms
   - Maintain funding compliance documentation

3. **Support Services**
   - Provide student support and case management
   - Offer digital learning platform access
   - Supply marketing and promotional materials
   - Facilitate employer connections for job placement

4. **Compliance & Reporting**
   - Maintain centralized student records
   - Coordinate required reporting to funding agencies
   - Provide compliance templates and guidance
   - Conduct periodic quality assurance reviews

### B. Program Holder Responsibilities

Program Holder agrees to:

1. **Program Delivery**
   - Provide quality instruction per approved curriculum
   - Maintain qualified, credentialed instructors
   - Ensure safe, accessible training facilities
   - Follow all applicable state and federal regulations

2. **Student Services**
   - Accept referred students meeting program prerequisites
   - Track and report student attendance
   - Communicate student progress and concerns
   - Provide reasonable accommodations as required by law
   - Issue certificates/credentials upon successful completion

3. **Compliance & Documentation**
   - Maintain current licenses, approvals, and insurance
   - Submit required reports on time
   - Notify Elevate of any compliance issues immediately
   - Participate in quality assurance reviews
   - Maintain student records per FERPA requirements

4. **Communication**
   - Respond to Elevate inquiries within 2 business days
   - Notify Elevate of schedule changes at least 2 weeks in advance
   - Report student withdrawals within 24 hours
   - Participate in monthly partner check-in calls
