- Process completions and certifications

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How quickly will I receive student referrals?**
A: Typically within 2-4 weeks of completing onboarding, depending on program demand.

**Q: What if a student drops out?**
A: Notify us immediately. Payment terms for withdrawals are outlined in the MOU.

**Q: Can I offer multiple programs?**
A: Yes! Each program requires separate approval and setup.
