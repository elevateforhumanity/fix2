## Program Overview

Launch your own business in 5 weeks! Learn entrepreneurship, digital marketing, LLC formation, and business planning with mentorship and startup support. Program includes business match stipend and laptop kit. Perfect for youth ages 16+ ready to explore self-employment and leadership pathways.

---

## Learning Objectives

1. Develop a viable business concept and plan
2. Register and structure a business entity (LLC, sole proprietorship)
3. Create digital marketing campaigns using social media
4. Build a professional website and online presence
5. Understand basic accounting and financial management
6. Apply for business licenses and permits
7. Pitch business ideas to potential investors or customers
8. Navigate small business resources and funding opportunities

---

## Course Outline

### Week 1: Entrepreneurship Foundations (8 hours)
- What is entrepreneurship?
- Identifying business opportunities
- Market research and customer discovery
- Business model canvas
- Legal structures (LLC, sole proprietor, partnership)
- Business name and branding basics

### Week 2: Business Planning & Finance (8 hours)
- Writing a business plan
- Financial projections and budgeting
- Startup costs and funding sources
- Pricing strategies
- Basic bookkeeping with QuickBooks
- Business bank accounts and credit

### Week 3: Digital Marketing & Social Media (8 hours)
- Social media marketing (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok)
- Content creation and storytelling
- Building a brand identity
- Website basics (Wix, Squarespace, WordPress)
- Email marketing fundamentals
- SEO and online visibility

### Week 4: Operations & Legal Compliance (4 hours)
- Registering your LLC in Indiana
- Obtaining EIN and business licenses
- Business insurance basics
- Contracts and agreements
- Intellectual property protection
- Tax obligations for small businesses

### Week 5: Pitch & Launch (4 hours)
- Elevator pitch development
- Presenting to investors or customers
- Launch planning and marketing
- Networking and partnerships
- Accessing small business resources
- Final business plan presentations

---

## Assessment Methods

- **Business Plan:** Complete written plan with financials
- **Pitch Presentation:** 5-minute business pitch to panel
- **Digital Marketing Portfolio:** Social media and website examples
- **Participation:** Active engagement in workshops
