## Program Overview

Become a licensed beauty instructor! This program prepares experienced cosmetologists and barbers to teach the next generation. Learn curriculum development, classroom management, and state licensing requirements. Perfect for those with 3+ years industry experience.

---

## Learning Objectives

1. Develop lesson plans and curriculum for beauty programs
2. Apply adult learning principles and teaching methods
3. Manage classroom and lab environments effectively
4. Assess student performance and provide feedback
5. Meet Indiana State Board instructor requirements
6. Demonstrate professional teaching practices
7. Integrate technology in beauty education
8. Prepare students for state licensing exams

- Learning styles and preferences
- Instructional design basics
- Curriculum development
- Lesson planning
- Educational psychology
- Diversity and inclusion in education

### Module 2: Teaching Methods & Strategies (60 hours)

- Hands-on instruction methods
- Group activities and projects
- Technology integration
- Visual aids and multimedia
- Differentiated instruction

- Behavior management strategies

- Time management

## Indiana Instructor Licensure

To obtain Indiana beauty instructor license, candidates must:
1. Hold current cosmetology or barber license
2. Have 3+ years professional experience
3. Complete approved instructor training program (this program)
4. Be at least 21 years old
5. Pass State Board instructor exam
6. Submit application and fees
7. Maintain continuing education
