# Tax Preparation & Financial Services
## Program Syllabus

**Provider:** Elevate for Humanity  
**ETPL Provider ID:** 10000949  
**Program Code:** TAX-PREP-FS  
**Duration:** 10 Weeks (150 Hours)  
**Delivery Method:** Hybrid (Online + In-Person Practicum)  
**Funding:** WIOA • WRG • Earn and Learn  

---

## Program Overview

State Certified Earn and Learn program preparing students for careers in tax preparation, bookkeeping, and financial services. Students earn multiple industry-recognized certifications including IRS VITA/TCE, QuickBooks Pro Advisor, and Microsoft 365 while gaining supervised practicum experience at IRS-approved VITA sites.

---

## Learning Objectives

Upon successful completion, students will be able to:

1. Prepare federal and state tax returns using IRS-approved software
2. Apply federal and state tax law to individual and small business returns
3. Perform bookkeeping functions using QuickBooks Online
4. Utilize Microsoft 365 applications for business operations
5. Provide financial literacy education to clients
6. Demonstrate professional ethics and client confidentiality
7. Navigate IRS resources and taxpayer assistance programs
8. Identify common tax credits and deductions (EITC, CTC, education credits)

---

## Course Outline

### Module 1: Program Orientation & Workplace Readiness (10 hours)
- Welcome and program expectations
- Career pathways in financial services
- Professional communication and customer service
- Time management and workplace ethics
- Digital literacy fundamentals

### Module 2: Federal Tax Law & Preparation (40 hours)
- Tax filing status and exemptions
- Income types and reporting (W-2, 1099, Schedule C)
- Standard vs. itemized deductions
- Tax credits (EITC, CTC, education, energy)
- Tax software navigation (TaxSlayer, TaxWise)
- IRS Publication 17 and Form 1040 series
- State tax law (Indiana IT-40)

### Module 3: IRS VITA/TCE Certification (20 hours)
- VITA program standards and procedures
- Intake and interview techniques
- Quality review and accuracy
- Taxpayer Bill of Rights
- Ethics and professional conduct
- Link & Learn Taxes training modules
- VITA certification exam preparation

### Module 4: QuickBooks Pro Advisor Training (30 hours)
- QuickBooks Online setup and navigation
- Chart of accounts and financial statements
- Accounts payable and receivable
- Bank reconciliation
- Payroll basics
- Reports and analytics
- QuickBooks ProAdvisor certification exam

### Module 5: Microsoft 365 Essentials (20 hours)
- Word: Business documents and mail merge
- Excel: Formulas, functions, and data analysis
- Outlook: Email management and calendaring
- Teams: Collaboration and communication
- OneDrive: Cloud storage and file sharing

### Module 6: Financial Literacy & Client Education (10 hours)
- Budgeting and money management
- Credit scores and reports
- Banking products and services
- Debt management strategies
- Savings and emergency funds
- Identity theft protection

### Module 7: Supervised Practicum at VITA Site (20 hours)
- Client intake and interview
- Tax return preparation under supervision
- Quality review process
- Client communication and follow-up
- Site operations and volunteer coordination
- Real-world application of skills

---

## Assessment Methods

- **Weekly Quizzes:** Module comprehension checks (70% passing)
- **IRS VITA Certification Exam:** Required for practicum placement
- **QuickBooks ProAdvisor Exam:** Industry certification
- **Microsoft 365 Skills Assessment:** Practical application test
- **Practicum Evaluation:** Supervisor assessment of client interactions
- **Final Portfolio:** Completed tax returns and case studies (anonymized)

---

## Grading Scale

- **A:** 90-100% - Excellent mastery
- **B:** 80-89% - Good understanding
- **C:** 70-79% - Satisfactory performance
- **F:** Below 70% - Does not meet standards

**Minimum passing grade:** 70% overall with VITA certification required
