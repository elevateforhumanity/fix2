**Delivery Method:** Hybrid (Online + Hands-On Skills)  
**Funding:** WIOA • WRG • Apprenticeship  

---

## Program Overview

DOL Federally Registered Apprenticeship! Earn OSHA 10, CPR, and Emergency Medical Responder (EMR) certifications in 4 weeks. Prepare for life-saving response roles in schools, workplaces, and emergency settings.

---

## Learning Objectives

1. Respond to medical emergencies with appropriate interventions
2. Perform CPR and use AED on adults, children, and infants
3. Apply OSHA safety standards in workplace settings
4. Assess and stabilize patients until EMS arrives
5. Control bleeding and treat shock
6. Recognize and respond to cardiac and respiratory emergencies
7. Document emergency incidents accurately

---

## Course Outline

### Week 1: OSHA 10 & Workplace Safety (20 hours)
- OSHA standards and regulations
- Hazard recognition and prevention
- Personal protective equipment (PPE)
- Fall protection and ladder safety
- Electrical safety
- Emergency action plans

### Week 2: CPR/AED & Basic Life Support (20 hours)
- Adult, child, and infant CPR
- AED operation
- Choking relief
- Rescue breathing
- Two-rescuer CPR
- Special resuscitation situations

### Week 3: Emergency Medical Responder I (20 hours)
- Patient assessment
- Airway management
- Bleeding control and bandaging
- Shock management
- Fractures and splinting
- Medical emergencies (heart attack, stroke, seizures)

### Week 4: Emergency Medical Responder II (20 hours)
- Trauma assessment and care
- Environmental emergencies
- Behavioral emergencies
- Childbirth and pediatric emergencies
- Mass casualty incidents
- Documentation and reporting

---

## Assessment Methods

- OSHA 10 exam (70% passing)
- CPR/AED skills demonstration
- EMR practical skills assessments
- Written final exam (75% passing)

---

## Certifications Earned

1. **OSHA 10-Hour General Industry**
2. **CPR/AED for Healthcare Providers** (American Heart Association)
3. **Emergency Medical Responder (EMR)** (NREMT eligible)
