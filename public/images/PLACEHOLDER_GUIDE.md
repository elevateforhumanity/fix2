# Image Placeholder Guide

## Current Status
All images in this directory should be replaced with properly licensed images.

## Safe Image Sources

### Free Commercial Use:
1. **Unsplash** (https://unsplash.com)
   - Free for commercial use
   - No attribution required
   - High quality

2. **Pexels** (https://pexels.com)
   - Free for commercial use
   - No attribution required
   - Good variety

3. **Pixabay** (https://pixabay.com)
   - Free for commercial use
   - No attribution required

### Paid Stock (Recommended):
1. **Shutterstock** (~$29/month)
2. **Adobe Stock** (~$29.99/month)
3. **iStock** (pay per image)

### Your Own Photos:
- Photos you take yourself
- Photos of your facilities
- Photos of students (with signed releases)

## Images to Replace

### Priority 1 (High Risk):
- Any images from Google Images
- Any images from other websites
- Any images without clear licensing

### Priority 2 (Medium Risk):
- Stock photos without documentation
- Images from unknown sources

### Priority 3 (Low Risk):
- Your own facility photos
- Partner-provided images (with permission)

## Replacement Process

1. **Identify Source**: Document where each image came from
2. **Check License**: Verify you have rights to use it
3. **Replace if Needed**: Use safe sources above
4. **Document**: Keep records of licenses

## Image Requirements

### Technical:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Size: Optimized for web (under 500KB)
- Dimensions: Appropriate for use case

### Legal:
- Commercial use allowed
- No attribution required (preferred)
- Perpetual license
