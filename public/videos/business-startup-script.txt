BUSINESS STARTUP PROGRAM SCRIPT — Elevate for Humanity
========================================================

Launch Your Own Business With Free Training, Real Certifications & Step-By-Step Support

========================================================

INTRO SECTION – "Start Your Business With Confidence"

At Elevate for Humanity, we make entrepreneurship possible for everyday people.
Whether you're starting a tax business, a barber shop, a beauty brand, a wellness clinic, a skilled trade business, or a service-based company, our Business Startup Program gives you everything you need to launch—training, tools, certifications, mentorship, and funding options.

This is not just a class.
It's a full business-in-a-box pathway backed by JRI, WRG, WIOA, and apprenticeship alignment.

========================================================

SECTION 1 – "Free or Funded Entrepreneurship Training"

Eligible students may receive full tuition assistance through:

• JRI – Funding & support for justice-involved individuals
• WRG – Workforce Ready Grants for high-demand career business paths
• WIOA – Federal training support, coaching, and small business development pathways
• Apprenticeships – Earn while you learn, then transition into owning your own business
• Stripe financing – Affirm, Klarna, Pay-in-4, and pay-in-full options

No matter your background, there is a pathway for you to start a business with little to no upfront cost.

========================================================

SECTION 2 – "Hybrid Training Built for Busy Entrepreneurs"

We offer a hybrid model designed for real life.
You'll learn:

• Online through our LMS
• In-person through workshops
• With hands-on demonstrations
• Through guided templates, scripts, contracts, and business tools

You can work, take care of family, and still build your business at your own pace.

========================================================

SECTION 3 – "What You Will Learn"

Inside the Business Startup Program, you will learn:

• How to structure and register your business (LLC, EIN, bank setup)
• How to price your services
• How to market, brand, and build your customer base
• How to automate your business using AI and software
• How to stay compliant (licenses, taxes, insurance, state requirements)
• How to create multiple revenue streams
• How to launch online and in-person
• How to scale from side hustle → legit business → hiring staff

Everything is step-by-step, guided, and broken down so even first-time entrepreneurs feel confident.

========================================================

SECTION 4 – "Learn While You Earn"

Your training includes:

• Internships and earn-to-learn opportunities
• Apprentice-level business training for service industries
• Real-world client practice
• Hands-on business-building projects
• Pathways to turn your internship experience into your first paying customers

You won't just learn business—you will start your business.

========================================================

SECTION 5 – "Stacked Credentials That Increase Income"

Students can add industry certifications depending on their track:

• Tax Preparer Certification
• Bookkeeping & Finance
• Beauty & Barber Apprenticeship + Shop Ownership Pathway
• Hospitality Business Management
• Healthcare Office Management
• Trades Business Startup
• Tech & Digital Entrepreneurship

Each credential gives you more earning power, more skills, and more ways to grow.

========================================================

SECTION 6 – "Build Generational Wealth, Not Debt"

Our mission is simple:
Help you build a business that creates stability, freedom, and long-term income.
Most students launch with less than $100 in startup costs because we help you:

• Set up free or low-cost tools
• Use AI for speed and automation
• Access grants, funding, and supportive services
• Create a launch plan that works for your situation

You deserve to build something real.
We're here to guide every step.

========================================================

SECTION 7 – "Call to Action"

Your business is waiting on you—not the other way around.
Apply now to join the Business Startup Program at ElevateForHumanity.org.
Free training, real support, and a proven blueprint to start strong.
