About Selfish Inc

Selfish Inc operates as a nonprofit organization in partnership with Elevate for Humanity, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to workforce development and community empowerment.

Our programs are designed to meet the unique needs of underserved communities, providing accessible, high-quality training and support services that lead to meaningful employment and career advancement.

We believe in the power of education and opportunity to transform lives and strengthen communities. Through our comprehensive approach, we address barriers to employment and create pathways to success.
