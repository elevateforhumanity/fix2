# BARBER APPRENTICESHIP SITE MOU
## Between Elevate for Humanity and ______________________

This Barber Apprenticeship Site Memorandum of Understanding ("MOU") is entered into by and between:

- **Elevate for Humanity** ("Elevate"), and  
- **________________________________________** ("Barber Shop" or "Site Partner"),

collectively the "Parties."

The purpose of this MOU is to outline how the Parties will work together to host **barber learners / apprentices** at the shop in a way that is safe, compliant, and beneficial to everyone involved.

---

## 1. Purpose

The Parties agree to collaborate to:

- Provide **hands-on barber training** and real shop exposure for approved Learners;
- Develop a **pipeline of barbers** who are shop-ready, license-ready, and customer-ready; and
- Align with **state barber board, apprenticeship, and workforce requirements** where applicable.

---

## 2. Scope of Collaboration

Under this MOU, the Barber Shop may host learners for:

- Barber apprenticeship hours;
- Supervised services on mannequins and models;
- Gradual, supervised work on live clients (as allowed by state rules); and
- Other related training activities approved by Elevate and the Barber Shop.

The specific schedules, cohorts, and details may be confirmed via email or program documents without reissuing this MOU.

---

## 3. Responsibilities of Elevate for Humanity

Elevate agrees to:

1. **Prepare Learners**
   - Provide or coordinate classroom and theory training following approved curriculum.
   - Set expectations with Learners around **attendance, professionalism, and hygiene**.

2. **Support the Shop**
   - Provide a **point of contact** for shop leadership to address concerns or questions.
   - Intervene when there are repeated **attendance, conduct, or performance** issues.
   - Assist with basic documentation needed for apprenticeship, workforce, or licensing pathways where Elevate is the sponsor or partner.

3. **Documentation**
   - Provide or support **hour logs, evaluation forms, or checklists** required for apprenticeship or licensing.
   - Use information from the shop to update Learner records and support board or workforce reporting.

---

## 4. Responsibilities of the Barber Shop / Site Partner

The Barber Shop agrees to:

1. **Environment & Professional Standards**
   - Maintain a shop environment that is **safe, clean, and professional**, in line with **state barber board and health regulations**.
   - Clearly communicate shop rules, expectations, and standards for **appearance, customer interaction, and sanitation**.

2. **Supervision & Training**
   - Ensure Learners are supervised by licensed barbers when performing services.
   - Allow Learners to:
     - Practice on mannequins and models;
     - Gradually perform supervised services on clients as permitted by law and policy.
   - Provide basic training in:
     - Station setup and breakdown;
     - Sanitation and disinfection;
     - Client greeting and customer service;
     - Shop flow and teamwork.

3. **Attendance, Hours & Feedback**
   - Accurately track **hours, days, and key activities** for each Learner.
   - Notify Elevate of:
     - Repeated tardiness, absences, or no call / no show;
     - Serious concerns about behavior, attitude, or safety.
   - Provide **feedback or simple evaluations** when requested to support progress and licensing requirements.

4. **Safety, Boundaries & Conduct**
   - Maintain a shop free from **harassment, discrimination, and abusive behavior**.
   - Avoid assigning Learners work that is:
     - Unsafe or not allowed by state rules;
     - Far outside the scope of training (for example, unrelated manual labor as their main activity).
   - Maintain professional boundaries with Learners and inform Elevate of serious incidents (fights, threats, harassment, substance use impacting safety, etc.).

---

## 5. Learner Status & Compensation

1. Unless separately and explicitly agreed:
   - Learners/apprentices in this context are **training participants**, not employees of Elevate.
   - Any **employment relationship** (if created) is between the Barber Shop and the individual Learner.

2. If the Barber Shop chooses to pay Learners:
   - The shop is responsible for **payroll, taxes, and employment law compliance** related to that decision.

---

## 6. Data, Photos, and Promotion

1. Data & Reporting:
   - The Barber Shop may share attendance, performance notes, and hour logs with Elevate to support licensing, apprenticeship, and workforce reporting.
   - Elevate will use these records only for legitimate program, compliance, and quality purposes.

2. Photos & Marketing:
   - The Barber Shop and Elevate agree to respect:
     - Photo consent from Learners and clients;
     - Restrictions around public posting for justice-involved individuals when shared by Elevate.
   - Any **co-branded marketing, heavy logo use, or media stories** featuring Elevate should be reviewed with Elevate in advance.

---

## 7. Term, Changes, and Ending the Relationship

1. **Term.**  
   This MOU is effective on the latest signature date below and continues for **one (1) year**, automatically renewing each year unless ended by either Party.

2. **Changes.**  
   Adjustments to details such as number of apprentices, schedules, or specific support can be made via email or written communication between the Parties.

3. **Termination.**  
   Either Party may end this MOU at any time, with or without cause, by written notice (email is acceptable). The Parties will:
   - Work together to **support current Learners** through a reasonable transition or closure where possible.

---

## 8. Non-Binding & No Guarantee

This MOU:

- Does **not** create a legal partnership, joint venture, or agency relationship.
- Does **not** guarantee a specific number of Learners, hours, or hires.
- Does **not** require either Party to pay money to the other, unless separately agreed in writing.
