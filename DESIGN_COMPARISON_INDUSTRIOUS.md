# Design Comparison: Industrious vs Elevate for Humanity

**Date:** December 17, 2024  
**Reference:** https://www.industriousoffice.com

---

## Executive Summary

**Industrious** is a premium coworking/office space company with a sophisticated, minimalist design focused on high-end B2B clients. Their design emphasizes:
- Clean, spacious layouts with generous whitespace
- Premium photography and professional imagery
- Clear value propositions with minimal text
- Strong visual hierarchy
- Elegant typography and spacing

**Your site** is more information-dense, focused on workforce development with multiple stakeholders (students, employers, agencies). It needs to balance:
- Educational content with conversion
- Multiple user journeys
- Compliance and credibility
- Accessibility and clarity

## KEY TAKEAWAYS

### What Industrious Does Better:
1. **Whitespace** - Generous padding everywhere
2. **Simplicity** - Minimal text, maximum impact
3. **Visual hierarchy** - Clear importance levels
4. **Consistency** - Spacing, colors, typography
5. **Premium feel** - High-quality imagery, clean design

### What You Should Keep:
1. **Video hero** - Engaging and modern
2. **Multiple user journeys** - Necessary for your audience
3. **Educational content** - Important for workforce development
4. **Compliance focus** - Required for your industry
5. **Accessibility** - Critical for public programs

### What to Change:
1. **Double your spacing** - Everywhere
2. **Simplify your copy** - Less text, more impact
3. **Better imagery** - Professional, consistent
4. **Clearer hierarchy** - One message per section
5. **More whitespace** - Let content breathe
