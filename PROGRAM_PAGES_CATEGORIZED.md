## Program Features

### All Programs Include:

- ✅ 100% free training (funded by WIOA, WRG, JRI, or employers)
- ✅ Industry-recognized certifications
- ✅ Hands-on training
- ✅ Job placement assistance
- ✅ Career support services
- ✅ Flexible scheduling options

### Program Durations:

- **Short-term (1-4 weeks):** CPR, DSP, Workforce Readiness
- **Medium-term (4-12 weeks):** CNA, Phlebotomy, HVAC, CDL, Tax Prep
- **Long-term (12+ weeks):** HVAC (advanced), Barber Apprenticeship

### Credentials Earned:

- State certifications (CNA, Barber, Esthetician)
- Industry certifications (HVAC EPA 608, CDL, Phlebotomy)
- Professional certificates (Business, Tax Prep, Peer Recovery)
