## Executive Summary

Elevate for Humanity (EFH) is an **ETPL-approved, WIOA-aligned Career Pathway Execution Partner** positioned to immediately serve SNAP E&T participants (ages 18-64) under the DOL-USDA alignment framework.

We provide:

- ✅ **80-hour monthly compliance tracking** (automated)
- ✅ **Industry-recognized credentials** in high-demand sectors
- ✅ **Work-based learning & DOL-registered apprenticeships**
- ✅ **Verified job placement** with wage documentation
- ✅ **Braided funding coordination** (WIOA + SNAP E&T)

**We do NOT**: Determine SNAP eligibility, administer benefits, or act as prime SNAP E&T agency.

**We DO**: Execute training, track hours, verify outcomes, and connect participants to employment.

## Funding Model

### How We Work with SNAP E&T

**We are a Third-Party Service Provider**, not a prime contractor.

**Funding flows through**:

- State SNAP E&T agency (FSSA)
- Local Workforce Boards (WorkOne)
- Community-based intermediaries

**We receive reimbursement for**:

- Training costs
- Support services
- Participant expenses
- Case management

**Braided Funding Model**:

- WIOA funds (for eligible participants)
- SNAP E&T funds (for SNAP recipients)
- WRG funds (for Indiana residents)
- JRI funds (for justice-involved)
- Employer contributions (for apprenticeships)

**We do NOT**:

- Charge participants tuition
- Determine SNAP eligibility
- Administer SNAP benefits
- Act as fiscal agent
