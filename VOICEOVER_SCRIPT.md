- **Voice**: Calm, warm, professional female or male voice
- **Tone**: Reassuring, hopeful, empowering
- **Background Music**: Soft, uplifting instrumental (piano/strings)
- **Duration**: 60-90 seconds
- **Format**: MP4 or MP3

**[Pause, sincere]**

Whether you're between jobs, coming back from a tough situation, or just ready for something better... we're here for you.

Give us a call at 317-314-3757, or just go to IndianaConnect.com right now.

Your next chapter starts here."
