## KEY PRINCIPLES OF WRAP-AROUND MODEL

### 1. Non-Disruptive

**We don't replace your business. We enhance it.**

- You keep your brand (or use ours, your choice)
- You keep your processes (or improve them with our tools)
- You keep your relationships (students, employers, partners)
- You keep your autonomy (within MOU guidelines for Program Holders)

### 2. Additive Value

**We add capabilities you don't have.**

- Technology (if you're using spreadsheets)
- Credentials (if you don't have ETPL)
- Automation (if you're doing manual work)
- Support (if you need technical help)

### 3. Flexible Integration

**You choose how much to use.**

**Minimum Integration:**

- Just use our credentials (Program Holder Network)
- Keep your existing systems
- Manual data entry to our platform

**Medium Integration:**

- Use our LMS for student management
- Keep your existing curriculum
- Automated reporting

**Maximum Integration:**

- Full platform adoption
- Migrate all students
- Use all features (mobile app, reporting, etc.)

### 4. Gradual Adoption

**You don't have to switch everything at once.**

**Phase 1: Pilot (Month 1-3)**

- Enroll 10-20 students on platform
- Keep existing students on old system
- Test and learn

**Phase 2: Expansion (Month 4-6)**

- Enroll 50% of new students on platform
- Migrate some existing students
- Refine processes

**Phase 3: Full Adoption (Month 7-12)**

- All new students on platform
- Migrate remaining students
- Retire old system (if desired)

## COMMON QUESTIONS

### Q: "Will I lose control of my business?"

**A:** No. You keep operational control. For Program Holder Network, you must follow MOU compliance standards (same as if you had your own ETPL credentials). For Independent Platform, you have full control.

### Q: "What if I want to leave?"

**A:**

- **Program Holder Network:** 30-day notice, we help you transition (apply for your own credentials or find another provider)
- **Independent Platform:** Month-to-month contract, cancel anytime, we export your data

### Q: "Can I keep my existing students?"

**A:** Yes! You can migrate them to the platform or keep them in your current system. Your choice.

### Q: "Do I have to change my curriculum?"

**A:** No. You keep your curriculum. The platform just hosts it and tracks student progress.

### Q: "What if my students don't like the platform?"

**A:** We offer training and support. If they still don't like it, you can adjust or exit (see contract terms).

### Q: "Can I use my own branding?"

**A:**

- **Program Holder Network:** Limited white-label (your name + "powered by" or similar)
- **Independent Platform:** Full white-label (100% your branding)

### Q: "What happens to my data if I leave?"

**A:** You own your data. We export it in standard formats (CSV, JSON) when you leave. No lock-in.
