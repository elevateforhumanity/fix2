## Important Requirements

### Maintain Eligibility
As a grantee, you must:
1. **Maintain account activity**: Log in at least once per month
2. **Meet quality standards**: 
   - Maintain 5% click-through rate (CTR) for 2 consecutive months
   - Have at least 2 ad groups per campaign
   - Have at least 2 ads per ad group
   - Use sitelink extensions
3. **Follow policies**: No single-word keywords, maintain relevant landing pages
4. **Annual survey**: Complete Google's annual program survey

### Policy Compliance
- Keywords must be at least 2 words (except for branded terms)
- Quality Score of 3+ for all keywords
- Geo-targeting allowed
- No commercial activity promotion
- Landing pages must be relevant and high-quality
