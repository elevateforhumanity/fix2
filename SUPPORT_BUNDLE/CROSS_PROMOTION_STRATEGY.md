# Cross-Promotion Strategy - Holistic Wellness Ecosystem

## Overview

Elevate for Humanity operates within a holistic wellness ecosystem that includes:
1. **Elevate for Humanity** - Career training & workforce development (501c3 nonprofit)
2. **Selfish Inc** - Mental wellness support (501c3 nonprofit)  
3. **Curvature Body Sculpting** - Physical wellness services (for-profit partner)

### Partnership Structure
- **10% of Curvature Body Sculpting sales** support Selfish Inc mental wellness programs
- **1% of Motivational Tea sales** (sold through Curvature) support educational initiatives
- All organizations work together to provide holistic support for individuals
- Partnership creates sustainable funding for nonprofit mental health services

**No cross-promotion** - Stay focused on specific program

## Google Ads Campaign Rules

### ✅ ALLOWED in Ad Copy
- Career training keywords
- Workforce development
- WIOA funding
- Job placement
- Specific programs (CNA, HVAC, etc.)
- Indianapolis/Marion County location

### ❌ NOT ALLOWED in Ad Copy
- Mental health/wellness keywords
- Body sculpting/physical wellness
- Curvature brand name
- Selfish Inc brand name (unless mental health ad)
- "Holistic" as primary keyword

### Landing Pages for Ads
**Primary** (90% of traffic):
- `/apply` - Application form
- `/programs` - All programs
- `/programs/cna` - Specific programs
- `/funding` - WIOA/WRG info
- `/programs/rise-up` - NRF training

**Secondary** (10% organic):
- `/selfish-inc` - Mental wellness
- `/` - Homepage (shows ecosystem)

**Primary Message**: "Mental wellness support for trauma, divorce, and addiction"
**Secondary Message**: "Part of holistic support including career and physical wellness"
**Call to Action**: "Get Support" / "Donate"

**Primary Message**: "Body wellness and confidence services"

**Call to Action**: "Learn More" / "Book Service"

## Compliance Checklist

### Google Ad Grants Compliance
- [ ] 100% of ad copy focuses on career training
- [ ] All ad landing pages primarily about workforce development
- [ ] Mental wellness mentioned as support service only
- [ ] No commercial services promoted in ads
- [ ] Cross-promotion limited to on-site content
- [ ] Primary nonprofit mission is clear and dominant

### IRS 501(c)(3) Compliance
- [ ] Nonprofit funds used for nonprofit purposes
- [ ] Commercial partnerships disclosed appropriately
- [ ] Primary mission (workforce development) maintained
- [ ] Supporting services enhance mission outcomes

### Ethical Standards
- [ ] Transparent about organizational relationships
- [ ] No misleading claims about services
- [ ] Clear distinction between nonprofit and for-profit
- [ ] User benefit is primary consideration

## Benefits of This Approach

### For Users
1. **Holistic Support**: Address career, mental health, and physical wellness
2. **Seamless Referrals**: Easy access to related services
3. **Complete Transformation**: Support for whole-person success

### For Organizations
1. **Shared Traffic**: Organic cross-referrals between sites
2. **Enhanced Outcomes**: Better success rates with holistic support
3. **Efficient Marketing**: Leverage each other's audiences
4. **Mission Alignment**: All services support individual empowerment

### For Google Ads Compliance
1. **Clear Primary Mission**: Career training is dominant focus
2. **Appropriate Cross-Promotion**: Supporting services enhance mission
3. **No Commercial Abuse**: For-profit service minimally mentioned
4. **User-Focused**: Cross-promotion serves user needs, not just marketing

- Review compliance with all policies

- Complete Google Ad Grants survey
- Review IRS 501(c)(3) compliance
- Assess overall ecosystem effectiveness
- Plan improvements for next year
