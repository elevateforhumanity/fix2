```
Hello and welcome to Elevate for Humanity! I'm excited to have you join our network of employer partners.
```

#### Video 2: Employer Portal Overview (15 min)
```
[SCRIPT]

Welcome to your Employer Portal walkthrough. Let me show you how to navigate and use all the features available to you.

When you first log in, you'll see your dashboard. This is your command center for all hiring activities.

At the top, you'll see key metrics: active job postings, new applications, scheduled interviews, and recent hires. These update in real-time.

On the left sidebar, you have your main navigation:
- Dashboard (where we are now)
- Job Postings
- Candidates
- Interviews
- Hired Employees
- Reports
- Settings

Let's click on Job Postings. Here you can see all your active, draft, and closed job postings. The green badge shows active postings that candidates can see. Yellow means draft - you're still working on it. Gray means closed.

Click "Post New Job" to create a posting. We'll cover that in detail in the next video.

Now let's look at Candidates. This is where you review applications. You can filter by program, date applied, status, and more. Each candidate card shows their photo, name, program completed, certifications earned, and a brief summary.

Click on any candidate to see their full profile: resume, certifications, work history, references, and assessment scores. You can message them directly, schedule an interview, or move them to the next stage.

The Interviews section shows your upcoming and past interviews. You can schedule new interviews, send calendar invites, and add notes after each interview.

Hired Employees tracks everyone you've hired through Elevate. We use this for outcome reporting and to measure program success. You'll update their status here: actively employed, promoted, left company, etc.

Reports give you insights into your hiring activity, time-to-hire, retention rates, and more. You can export these for your records.

Settings is where you manage your company profile, notification preferences, and team members who have access to the portal.

That's your employer portal overview. In the next video, we'll walk through posting your first job opening step by step.

[END SCRIPT]
```

## üé¨ How to Create Videos with Each Tool

### Using Synthesia (Recommended):

1. **Sign up** at https://www.synthesia.io
2. **Choose "Create Video"**
3. **Select Template**: Choose "Training Video" or "Tutorial"
4. **Choose Avatar**: Pick a professional-looking avatar
5. **Paste Script**: Copy the script from above
6. **Customize**:
   - Add your logo
   - Choose brand colors
   - Add screen recordings if needed
7. **Generate**: Click "Generate Video"
8. **Download**: Export as MP4

### Using Pictory:

1. **Sign up** at https://pictory.ai
2. **Choose "Script to Video"**
3. **Paste Script**: Copy the full script
4. **Select Template**: Choose professional template
5. **AI Processing**: Pictory automatically:
   - Selects relevant stock footage
   - Adds voiceover
   - Creates scenes
   - Adds captions
6. **Edit**: Adjust timing, footage, or voiceover
7. **Export**: Download MP4

### Using Loom (For Platform Walkthroughs):

1. **Install** Loom desktop app or Chrome extension
2. **Click Record**
3. **Choose**: Screen + Camera or Screen Only
4. **Navigate**: Walk through the platform while explaining
5. **Stop Recording**: Loom automatically uploads
6. **Edit**: Trim, add call-to-actions
7. **Share**: Get embed link or download

## üì§ Uploading Videos to Your Site

### Option 1: YouTube (Recommended)
1. Upload videos to YouTube (can be unlisted)
2. Get embed code
3. Replace placeholder URLs in orientation pages
4. Example: `https://www.youtube.com/embed/YOUR_VIDEO_ID`

### Option 2: Vimeo
1. Upload to Vimeo
2. Get embed code
3. Replace placeholder URLs
4. Example: `https://player.vimeo.com/video/YOUR_VIDEO_ID`

### Option 3: Direct Upload (Supabase Storage)
1. Upload MP4 to Supabase Storage
2. Get public URL
3. Use HTML5 video player
4. Example: `<video src="YOUR_SUPABASE_URL" controls />`
