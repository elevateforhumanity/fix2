# ELEVATE FOR HUMANITY: PLATFORM SNAPSHOT & GAP CHECKLIST

## Overview

Elevate For Humanity is a workforce training and learning platform designed for adult learners, working families, and returning citizens. It combines:

- A public-facing marketing site
- A learning management system (LMS)
- Funding and case-manager workflows
- Employer and program-holder portals

This checklist compares Elevate For Humanity's current features to leading platforms like Coursera, LearnWorlds, and WorkKeys, with a focus on workforce and reentry alignment.
