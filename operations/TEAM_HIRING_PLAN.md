## HIRE #1: PROGRAM DIRECTOR (Month 3)

### Job Description

**Title:** Program Director  
**Reports To:** Founder  
**Salary:** $60,000-$80,000/year  
**Location:** Remote (Indiana preferred)  
**Start Date:** Month 3

**Role:**
Oversee program holder relationships and ensure compliance with Indiana DWD requirements.

**Responsibilities:**

- Manage program holder onboarding (5-10 per quarter)
- Monitor compliance (daily checks, weekly reviews)
- Conduct quarterly performance reviews
- Handle escalations and issues
- Build relationships with Indiana DWD
- Ensure all reports submitted on time
- Train program holders on compliance
- Develop best practices documentation

**Requirements:**

- 3+ years workforce development experience
- WIOA/ETPL knowledge required
- Indiana DWD experience preferred
- Relationship management skills
- Compliance expertise
- Strong communication
- Detail-oriented
- Self-starter

**Nice to Have:**

- Former ETPL provider experience
- Indiana DWD staff experience
- Nonprofit management experience
- Training/teaching experience

**Interview Questions:**

1. Tell me about your experience with WIOA/ETPL programs.
2. How do you ensure compliance with complex regulations?
3. Describe a time you managed multiple stakeholder relationships.
4. How would you handle a program holder who's consistently late on reports?
5. What's your experience with Indiana DWD?

**Where to Post:**

- Indeed (workforce development jobs)
- LinkedIn (target Indiana DWD connections)
- NAWB (National Association of Workforce Boards)
- NAWDP (National Association of Workforce Development Professionals)
- Indiana Workforce Development Board websites

## HIRING PROCESS

### Step 1: Job Posting (Week 1)

- Write job description
- Post on job boards
- Share on LinkedIn
- Reach out to network

### Step 2: Resume Review (Week 2)

- Review applications
- Shortlist top 10 candidates
- Send screening questions

### Step 3: Phone Screens (Week 3)

- 15-minute calls with top 5
- Assess basic fit
- Narrow to top 3

### Step 4: Interviews (Week 4)

- 60-minute video interviews
- Ask behavioral questions
- Assess skills and culture fit
- Check references

### Step 5: Offer (Week 5)

- Extend offer to top candidate
- Negotiate if needed
- Send offer letter
- Background check

### Step 6: Onboarding (Week 6)

- Send equipment
- Set up accounts
- Schedule training
- Assign first projects

**Total Hiring Timeline: 6 weeks per role**
