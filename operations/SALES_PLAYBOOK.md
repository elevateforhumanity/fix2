## DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT (30 minutes)

```

I'm [Your Name] from Elevate for Humanity. We help workforce training organizations access WIOA funding and automate compliance.

```

### Discovery Questions (15 min)

```
1. "Tell me about your organization. What's your mission?"

2. "What workforce training programs do you offer?"

3. "How many students do you serve per year?"

4. "Walk me through your enrollment process. What does that look like?"

5. "How do you handle Indiana DWD reporting? What's that process like?"

6. "What technology do you currently use? LMS? Student management?"

7. "What's working well for you? What's frustrating?"

8. "If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing, what would it be?"
```

```
"Thanks for sharing that. Based on what you've told me, I think we can help with [specific pain point].

1. Program Holder Network - if you don't have ETPL credentials
2. Independent Platform - if you already have credentials

Which sounds more relevant to you?"
```

```

- One missed deadline = removal from ETPL

- Using spreadsheets or old LMS
- No mobile access for students
- Manual processes everywhere

Sound familiar?"
```

## OBJECTION HANDLING

### "Too expensive"

```
"I understand budget is important. Let's look at ROI:

With WIOA funding access, your students can get $3K-$10K in tuition assistance.

If you enroll just 10 more students per year because of this, that's $30K-$100K in additional revenue.

The platform pays for itself many times over.

Plus, we offer a FREE 90-day pilot. Try it risk-free."
```

### "Need to think about it"

```
"Absolutely, this is an important decision.

What specific concerns do you have? Let's address them now so you have all the information you need.

Is it pricing? Features? Implementation? Something else?"
```

### "Need board approval"

```
"That makes sense. What information does your board need?

I can provide:
- Board presentation (10 slides)
- ROI analysis
- References from similar organizations
- Legal review of MOU

What would be most helpful?"
```

### "Already have a system"

```
"Great! What system are you using?

Our platform wraps around your existing operations. You don't have to replace everything.

Many customers start with just compliance automation, then add more features over time.

What's your biggest pain point with your current system?"
```

### "Not sure about credential sharing"

```
"That's a fair concern. We're getting legal validation now from an attorney who specializes in education law.

Also, we offer an Independent Platform option where you keep your own credentials and just license our technology.

Would that be a better fit?"
```
